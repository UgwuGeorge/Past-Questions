# NECO IRS Past Questions (2016)

## Objectives

**1.** Hajjatul Wada of Prophet Muhammad was delivered in 
   A) 632
   B) 630
   C) 628
   D) 631
   **Answer: A**

**2.** Ibadah refers to  
   A) worship of ancestors
   B) worship of the Prophet
   C) worship of the caliphs
   D) worship of Allah
   **Answer: D**

**3.** The reason Q.  2: 219 forbids wine ad gambling is because  
   A) they lead one to greater sin than gain
   B) they lead to poverty ultimately
   C) they lead to extravagance and intoxication
   D) they make a Muslim to abandon religion
   **Answer: A**

**4.** Shirk means ____  
   A) monotheism
   B) polytheism
   C) henotheism
   D) syncretism
   **Answer: B**

**5.** The final and total prohibition of intoxicants is contained in Surah  
   A) al-Baqarah
   B) an-Nisa
   C) al-Maidah
   D) al- I a
   **Answer: C**

**6.** The spokesman of the Muslims before Emperor Negus was 
   A) Jaafar
   B) Al-As
   C) Abu Musa
   D) Masa Musa
   **Answer: A**

**7.** If a Muslim prays five times daily on a regular basis, how many times will he have prayed a regular 
   A) 1825
   B) 1835
   C) 1835
   D) 1845
   **Answer: A**
