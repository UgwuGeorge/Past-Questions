## Objectives

**1.** Hajjatul Wada of Prophet Muhammad was delivered in 
   A) 632
   B) 630
   C) 628
   D) 631
   **Answer: A**

**2.** If Alhaji Musa Ayuba invested N10, 000: 00 in a business and made a profit of N2, 000: 00 at the
   A) N215.00
   B) N100: 00
   C) N50:00
   D) N1000:00
   **Answer: C**

**3.** Zakat is paid on the following except____  
   A) cash
   B) cattle
   C) slaves
   D) all of the above
   **Answer: C**

**4.** The reason Q.  2: 219 forbids wine ad gambling is because  
   A) they lead one to greater sin than gain
   B) they lead to poverty ultimately
   C) they lead to extravagance and intoxication
   D) they make a Muslim to abandon religion
   **Answer: A**

**5.** When Prophet Muhammad got to Yathrib, he had to first and foremost settle the feud between  
   A) Aws and Khazraj
   B) banu Nadir and Mecca
   C) Mecca and Medina
   D) Aws and Nadir
   **Answer: A**

**6.** Shirk means ____  
   A) monotheism
   B) polytheism
   C) henotheism
   D) syncretism
   **Answer: B**

**7.** Wudu means  
   A) ablution
   B) dry ablution
   C) water ablution
   D) wet ablution
   **Answer: B**

**8.** The reason Mecca suwar lay emphasis on oneness of Allah is because  
   A) it was the only way to call Mecca to Islam
   B) it is the only article of faith
   C) it is the only article of peace between Prophet Muhammad and the Mecca
   D) the pre-Islamic Mecca were idolatrous
   **Answer: D**
