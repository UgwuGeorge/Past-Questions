# NECO Government Past Questions (2013)

## Objectives

**1.** To contest an election, a candidate must be a member of a
   A) campaign organization.
   B) civil society organization.
   C) national assembly
   D) political party.
   **Answer: D**

**2.** Which of the following is a function of an electoral commission?
   A) Contesting elections
   B) Organizing and conducting elections
   C) Organizing political parties
   D) Settling disputes among political parties
   **Answer: B**

**3.** Which of these is a feature of Nigeria federalism?
   A) Conflict of authority does not arise
   B) Minority groups are equally benefited
   C) Parliamentary supremacy
   D) Secession is constitutionally forbidden
   **Answer: B**

**4.** Before a candidate is declared winner under absolute majority system of voting, the candidate must score more than ___ of the total vote cast.
   A) half
   B) one - quarter
   C) one - third
   D) three - quarter
   **Answer: A**

**5.** How many members of the security council of the United Nations (UN) exercise veto power?
   A) Eight
   B) Five
   C) Four
   D) Six
   **Answer: B**

**6.** Which of the following is NOT a feature of a state?
   A) Government
   B) Nationality
   C) Sovereignty
   D) Territory
   **Answer: B**

**7.** Revenue allocation means the sharing of the
   A) national cake among the people
   B) wealth of a nation among different levels of government
   C) wealth of a nation among the state governments for specific purposes
   D) wealth of a nation between the federal and local governments
   **Answer: B**

**8.** The Commonwealth of Nations consists of states formerly under ___colony.
   A) Belgium
   B) British
   C) French
   D) Portuguese
   **Answer: B**
