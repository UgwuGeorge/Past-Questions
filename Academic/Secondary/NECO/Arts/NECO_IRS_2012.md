# NECO IRS Past Questions (2012)

## Objectives

**1.** Talaq al-
   A) irrevocable
   B) revocable
   C) impermissible
   D) detestable
   **Answer: A**

**2.** The reason Mecca suwar lay emphasis on oneness of Allah is because  
   A) it was the only way to call Mecca to Islam
   B) it is the only article of faith
   C) it is the only article of peace between Prophet Muhammad and the Mecca
   D) the pre-Islamic Mecca were idolatrous
   **Answer: D**

**3.** The final and total prohibition of intoxicants is contained in Surah  
   A) al-Baqarah
   B) an-Nisa
   C) al-Maidah
   D) al- I a
   **Answer: C**

**4.** The spokesman of the Muslims before Emperor Negus was 
   A) Jaafar
   B) Al-As
   C) Abu Musa
   D) Masa Musa
   **Answer: A**

**5.** When Prophet Muhammad got to Yathrib, he had to first and foremost settle the feud between  
   A) Aws and Khazraj
   B) banu Nadir and Mecca
   C) Mecca and Medina
   D) Aws and Nadir
   **Answer: A**

**6.** Which one of the following is not associated with shirk _____ 
   A) idol worship
   B) trinity
   C) monotheism
   D) henotheism
   **Answer: C**

**7.** Walam yakun lahu Kufuwan Ahadun is_____  
   A) the second ayat of the 112th surah
   B) sixth ayat of the 111th surah
   C) fourth ayat of the 112th surah
   D) seventh ayat of the 114th surah
   **Answer: C**

**8.** Literally, Boko Haram means  
   A) book is forbidden
   B) education is forbidden
   C) Western education is forbidden
   D) school is forbidden
   **Answer: C**
