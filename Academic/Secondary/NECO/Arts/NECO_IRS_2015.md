   A) go to China to seek knowledge

**2.** The reason Q.  2: 219 forbids wine ad gambling is because  
   A) they lead one to greater sin than gain
   B) they lead to poverty ultimately
   C) they lead to extravagance and intoxication
   D) they make a Muslim to abandon religion

**3.** Uthman B. Affan reigned between  
   A) 644 and 656
   B) 657 and 688
   C) 655 and 657

**4.** Suratul-Kawthar was revealed following the _____ of the Mecca

**5.** Shirk means ____

   C) henotheism

   B) it purifies the Muslims
   C) it makes all Muslims equal
   D) it distinguishes pilgrims by country of origin
