# NECO CRS Past Questions (2020)

## Objectives

**1.** “Live as free men, yet without using your freedom as pretest for evil” Peter by this text was calling on Christians to
   A) resist the devil always.
   B) do what is right
   C) fight for their freedom
   D) endure pain while suffering
   **Answer: B**

**2.** As they were sharing Christ’s sufferings, Peter advised Christians to
   A) be prayerful
   B) rejoice
   C) be trustful
   D) persevere
   **Answer: B**

**3.** In the Epistle to the Romans, justification is received through 
   A) faith in Jesus
   B) moral uprightness
   C) observance of the law.
   D) giving of alms
   **Answer: A**

**4.** Joseph was of a good behaviour because he
   A) was loved and pampered by his father
   B) interacted well with his prision inmates
   C) rejected Potiphar’s wife proposal
   D) served diligently in Potiphar’s house
   **Answer: D**

**5.** According to Paul, ‘As sin reigned in death, grace also might reign through righteousness to
   A) faith in God’.
   B) the knowledge of God’.
   C) eternal life
   D) the glory of the father’.
   **Answer: C**

**6.** "I am the resurrection and the life, he who believes in me, though he dies, yet shall he live". Jesus directed this statement to
   A) Peter
   B) Martha
   C) Mary
   D) Lazarus
   **Answer:  B**

**7.** What advice did Peter give to the elders of the church in their relationship with the younger members? 
   A) Work as if being under external constraint
   B) Work without a domineering spirit
   C) Work with a desire for material gains
   D) Control their flocks with firm hands
   **Answer: B**

**8.** What advice did Peter give to the elders of the church in their relationship with the younger members?
   A) Work as if being under external constraint
   B) Work without a domineering spirit
   C) Work with a desire for material gains
   D) Control their flocks with firm hands
   **Answer: B**

**9.** John the Baptist describes Jesus to his two disciples as the 
   A) beloved Son of God
   B) great one of Israel
   C) holy one of God
   D) lamb of God.
   **Answer: D**
