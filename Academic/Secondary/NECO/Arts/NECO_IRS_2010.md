# NECO IRS Past Questions (2010)

## Objectives

**1.** Literally, Boko Haram means  
   A) book is forbidden
   B) education is forbidden
   C) Western education is forbidden
   D) school is forbidden
   **Answer: C**

**2.** Lakum dinukum wa Liya dinin means  
   A) religion is good
   B) to you is my religion and to me yours
   C) to you is your religion and to me is my religion
   D) my religion is better than yours
   **Answer: C**

**3.** Zakat is paid on the following except____  
   A) cash
   B) cattle
   C) slaves
   D) all of the above
   **Answer: C**

**4.** ?eek k o ledge, e e  u to Chi a   ea s ____ 
   A) go to China to seek knowledge
   B) knowledge is found in China
   C) go at any length to seek knowledge
   D) knowledge must be sought in china
   **Answer: C**

**5.** If a Muslim prays five times daily on a regular basis, how many times will he have prayed a regular 
   A) 1825
   B) 1835
   C) 1835
   D) 1845
   **Answer: A**

**6.** Shirk means ____  
   A) monotheism
   B) polytheism
   C) henotheism
   D) syncretism
   **Answer: B**

**7.** Hajjatul Wada of Prophet Muhammad was delivered in 
   A) 632
   B) 630
   C) 628
   D) 631
   **Answer: A**

**8.** Talaq al-
   A) irrevocable
   B) revocable
   C) impermissible
   D) detestable
   **Answer: A**
