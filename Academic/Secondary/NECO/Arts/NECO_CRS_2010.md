# NECO CRS Past Questions (2010)

## Objectives

**1.** Joel and Abijah were the sons of
   A) Eli.
   B) Saul
   C) Samuel
   D) David.
   **Answer: C**

**2.** A good citizen according to Peter is one 
   A) obeys the law of the state and provides for the poor
   B) provides social services for the needy.
   C) obeys the law of the state and fulfils his obligations.
   D) helps to finance the government at all times.
   **Answer: C**

**3.** The meaning of Ephphatha is 
   A) be opened
   B) be still
   C) be silent
   D) little girl, arise
   **Answer: A**

**4.** To whom was the body of Jesus Christ given for burial after His death? 
   A) Nicodemus the Pharisee
   B) Simon of Cyrene
   C) Simon the disciple
   D) Joseph of Arimathea.
   **Answer: B**

**5.** How old was Joshua before he died?
   A) 95 years
   B) 98 years
   C) 110 years
   D) 111 years
   **Answer: C**

**6.** Life in the Early Church was characterized by
   A) fasting and almsgiving
   B) prophetic activities
   C) communal spirit
   D) honesty and peace
   **Answer: C**

**7.** Peter encourage the church to humble themselves before  God so that He would
   A) exonerate them
   B) not punish them
   C) exalt them
   D) not disown them
   **Answer: C**

**8.** During the creative activity of God, He made the beasts
   A) in His own image
   B) to multiply on the earth
   C) according to their kinds
   D) to serve the needs of man
   **Answer: C**

**9.** “... you brood of vipers!  who warned you to flee from the wrath to come?” The “wrath to come” here refers to the 
   A) day of confusion.
   B) judgement day.
   C) rejection by God
   D) burning furnace.
   **Answer: B**

**10.** “How is it that you a Jew ask a drink of me...” This statement was made by the
   A) Gentile woman.
   B) woman of Samaria.
   C) possessed woman.
   D) woman of Galilee
   **Answer: B**
