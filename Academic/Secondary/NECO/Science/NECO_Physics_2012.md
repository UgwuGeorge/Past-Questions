**5.** The action of blotting paper on ink is due to
   A) diffusion
   B) osmosis
   C) capillarity
   D) surface tension
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: Capillarity occurs when adhesive forces (between ink and paper fibers) are stronger than cohesive forces (within the ink itself), causing the ink to climb up the tiny spaces in the paper.*

**7.** The pin-hole camera produces a less sharply defined image when the
   A) pin-hole is larger
   B) illumination is less
   C) screen is further from the pin-hole
   D) object is further from the pin-hole
   **Answer: A**
   *Explanation: The pin-hole camera produces a less sharply defined (blurred) image when the pin-hole is larger because multiple light rays from each object point can pass through, creating overlapping light circles on the screen.*

**9.** In the study of Physics, temperature and heat are often confused with each other. Which of the following statements correctly defines these two elements?
   A) Temperature is a measure of the average kinetic energy of the molecules of a substance
   B) Heat is a measure of the total kinetic energy of the molecules in a system
   C) Different materials require different amounts of heat to cause a given change in temperature
   D) All of the above
   **Answer: D**
   *Explanation: All of the statements correctly define temperature and heat: (A) temperature measures average molecular kinetic energy, (B) heat relates to total kinetic energy of the system, and (C) different materials have different heat requirements for temperature changes.*
