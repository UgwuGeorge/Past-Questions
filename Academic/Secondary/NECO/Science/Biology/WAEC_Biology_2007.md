# NECO Biology Past Questions (2007)

## Objectives

**1.** The smallest unit of classification is the
   A) Kingdom
   B) Phylum
   C) Species
   D) Genus
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: Species is the basic unit of classification.*

**2.** Which of the following is a sex-linked disease?
   A) Diabetes
   B) Color blindness
   C) Hypertension
   D) Arthritis
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Color blindness is an X-linked recessive disorder.*

**3.** The part of the brain responsible for balance is the
   A) Cerebrum
   B) Cerebellum
   C) Medulla
   D) Hypothalamus
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: The cerebellum coordinates movement and balance.*

**4.** In plants, the vascular tissue that transports water is the
   A) Phloem
   B) Xylem
   C) Cambium
   D) Pith
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Xylem conducts water and minerals.*

**5.** The type of immunity where antibodies are transferred is called
   A) Active immunity
   B) Passive immunity
   C) Natural immunity
   D) Acquired immunity
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Passive immunity involves receiving antibodies from another source.*

**6.** Which blood group is the universal recipient?
   A) A
   B) B
   C) AB
   D) O
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: AB can receive blood from any type.*

**7.** The process of change from larva to adult is called
   A) Metamorphosis
   B) Metamorphosis
   C) Growth
   D) Differentiation
   **Answer: A**
   *Explanation: Metamorphosis is the transformation from larval to adult form.*

**8.** In human genetics, if a child has blood group O, the parents must have
   A) A and B alleles only
   B) O allele only
   C) At least one O allele
   D) No A or B alleles
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: To have type O, both parents must contribute an O allele.*

**9.** Which organ is affected by the disease hepatitis?
   A) Heart
   B) Lungs
   C) Liver
   D) Kidneys
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: Hepatitis is inflammation of the liver.*

**10.** The part of the plant that absorbs water is the
   A) Stem
   B) Leaf
   C) Root hair
   D) Flower
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: Root hairs increase surface area for water absorption.*
