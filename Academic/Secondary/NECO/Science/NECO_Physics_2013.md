**9.** A car starts from rest and covers a distance of 40 m in 10 s. Calculate the magnitude of its acceleration.
   A) 3.20 ms⁻²
   B) 0.25 ms⁻²
   C) 0.80 ms⁻²
   D) 4.00 ms⁻²
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: nitial velocity, u = 0 m/s (starts from rest)
Distance, s = 40 m
Time, t = 10 s
Acceleration, a = ?

Solution:
Using the equation of motion:
s = ut + ½at²
Step 1: Substitute the known values
40 = (0)(10) + ½a(10)²
40 = 0 + ½a(100)
40 = 50a
Step 2: Solve for acceleration
a = 40/50
a = 0.80 m/s²*
