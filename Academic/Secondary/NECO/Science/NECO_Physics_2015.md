**8.** The average translational kinetic energy of gas molecules depends on
   A) Temperature only
   B) Number of moles and temperature
   C) Pressure and temperature
   D) Pressure only
   **Answer: A**
   *Explanation: The average translational kinetic energy of gas molecules depends on temperature only. This is expressed as Average KE = (3/2)kT, where temperature is the sole determining factor.*

**9.** When the temperature of a liquid is increased, its surface tension?
   A) increases
   B) decreases
   C) remain constant
   D) increases then decreases
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: When the temperature of a liquid is increased, its surface tension decreases due to weakening of intermolecular cohesive forces.*
