Thermometer is used to measure temperature
Hydrometer is used to measure relative density of a liquid

**9.** If the rate of evaporation is y, and the rate of condensation is x, then, a vapour is saturated when
   A) x > y
   B) x < y
   C) x = y
   D) x ≤ y
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: A vapour is saturated when the rate of condensation (x) equals the rate of evaporation (y), establishing a dynamic equilibrium where there is no net change in the amount of vapour present.*
   *(Note: Closest available year from ALOC database: 2024)*

**10.** If silicon is doped with phosphorus, what type of semiconductor material will be formed?
   A) Zener material
   B) P-n junction
   C) n-type
   D) p-type
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: When silicon is doped with phosphorus, an n-type semiconductor material is formed due to the extra free electrons provided by phosphorus atoms.*
