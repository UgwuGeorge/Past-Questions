# NECO Chemistry Past Questions (2020)

## Objectives

**1.** Which of the following is not a compound?
   A) Water
   B) Carbon dioxide
   C) Oxygen
   D) Sodium chloride
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: Oxygen (O2) is an element, not a compound.*

**2.** The mass number of an atom is the sum of
   A) Protons and electrons
   B) Neutrons and electrons
   C) Protons and neutrons
   D) Protons, neutrons, and electrons
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: Mass number = protons + neutrons.*

**3.** Which of the following gases is lighter than air?
   A) CO2
   B) O2
   C) H2
   D) Cl2
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: H2 (molar mass 2) is lighter than air (≈29).*

**4.** The process of converting fats to soap is called
   A) Hydrolysis
   B) Saponification
   C) Esterification
   D) Polymerization
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Saponification is the hydrolysis of fats with alkali.*

**5.** Which of the following is a Lewis acid?
   A) NH3
   B) HCl
   C) BF3
   D) H2O
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: BF3 accepts an electron pair (Lewis acid).*

**6.** The colour of methyl orange in acidic medium is
   A) Yellow
   B) Red
   C) Orange
   D) Colorless
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Methyl orange turns red in acid.*

**7.** Which of the following is a property of noble gases?
   A) Highly reactive
   B) Colourless
   C) Form compounds easily
   D) Radioactive
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Noble gases are generally colorless and inert.*

**8.** The reaction between an acid and a base is called
   A) Oxidation
   B) Reduction
   C) Neutralization
   D) Decomposition
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: Acid + Base → Salt + Water (Neutralization).*

**9.** Which of the following is a greenhouse gas?
   A) N2
   B) O2
   C) CH4
   D) H2
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: Methane (CH4) is a potent greenhouse gas.*

**10.** The systematic name of KClO3 is
   A) Potassium chlorate
   B) Potassium chloride
   C) Potassium chlorite
   D) Potassium perchlorate
   **Answer: A**
   *Explanation: KClO3 is potassium chlorate.*
