   B) 60° and 60°
   C) 90° and 90°
   D) 90° and 0°
   **Answer: D**
   *Explanation: At the Earth's north magnetic pole, the angle is 90° (needle vertical), and at the magnetic equator, the angle is 0° (needle horizontal).*
