# NECO Commerce Past Questions (2013)

## Objectives

**1.** Calculate the total liabilities.
   A) 40,000
   B) 50,000
   C) 55,000
   D) 57,000
   **Answer: C**

**2.** Which of the following is NOT a characteristic of labour? It is
   A) fixed in supply
   B) geographically mobile
   C) man made
   D) perishable in nature
   **Answer: A**

**3.** A law made by a country to prohibit importation of certain goods into the country is called _____ laws.
   A) export
   B) import
   C) protectionist
   D) tariff
   **Answer: C**

**4.** One of the problems associated with home trade is the
   A) absence of currency exchange
   B) better knowledge of the markets
   C) inadequacy of storage facilities
   D) proximity of buyers and sellers
   **Answer: C**

**5.** Obtaining feedback on the quality and performance of competing products of different manufacturers, explains the retailer’s function of
   A) after sales service
   B) granting to credits to consumers
   C) market research
   D) stocking variety of goods
   **Answer: D**

**6.** Social responsibilities of a business to the consumers 	include the following, EXCEPT
   A) educating the customers
   B) ensuring truth in advertisement
   C) granting credit facilities
   D) providing of quality product
   **Answer: C**

**7.** The following are merits of industrial integration, EXCEPT
   A) controlling output and price
   B) eliminating waste
   C) leading to monopoly
   D) preventing over production
   **Answer: C**
