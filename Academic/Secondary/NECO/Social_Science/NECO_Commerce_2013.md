# NECO Commerce Past Questions (2013)

## Objectives

**1.** A manufacturer is said to have performed all the necessary marketing functions in the distribution channel when the goods move from
   A) manufacturer ? consumer
   B) manufacturer ? retailer ? consumer
   C) manufacturer ? wholesaler ? retailer ? consumer
   D) manufacturer ? wholesaler ?jobber ? retailer ?consumer
   **Answer: C**

**2.** One of the problems associated with home trade is the
   A) absence of currency exchange
   B) better knowledge of the markets
   C) inadequacy of storage facilities
   D) proximity of buyers and sellers
   **Answer: C**

**3.** Which of the following does NOT appear on a cheque on presentation for payment?
   A) Amount in words and figures
   B) Date of withdrawal
   C) Name of the cashier
   D) Name of the payee
   **Answer: C**

**4.** An instruction from a current account holder requesting to pay a named person or organization a. specified amount is 		called
   A) bankers order
   B) certified cheque
   C) internal transfer
   D) money order
   **Answer: B**

**5.** Obtaining feedback on the quality and performance of competing products of different manufacturers, explains the retailer’s function of
   A) after sales service
   B) granting to credits to consumers
   C) market research
   D) stocking variety of goods
   **Answer: D**

**6.** The aspect of production which provides goods and services for immediate consumption is called ___ production.
   A) direct
   B) indirect
   C) primary
   D) secondary
   **Answer: A**

**7.** The actual gain made by a business after all expenses has been deducted is called
   A) gross profit
   B) margin
   C) mark-up
   D) net profit
   **Answer: D**

**8.** The final link in the chain of distribution is the
   A) agent
   B) consumer
   C) manufacturer
   D) retailer
   **Answer: B**

**9.** Another name for trade discount is _________ discount,
   A) cash
   B) functional
   C) quality
   D) quantity
   **Answer: D**
