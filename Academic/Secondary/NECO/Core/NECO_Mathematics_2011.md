   A) 16
   B) 14
   C) 12
   D) 10
   **Answer: A**
   *Explanation: Range = Highest Number - Lowest Number

Mode is the number with highest occurrence
10, 9, 10, 9, 8, 7, 7, 10, 8, 4, 6,, 9, 10, 9, 7, 10, 6, 5

Range = 10 − 4 = 6

Mode = 10

Sum of range and mode = range + mode = 6 + 10

= 16*
