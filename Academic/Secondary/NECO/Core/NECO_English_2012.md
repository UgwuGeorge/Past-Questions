**3.** Choose the option nearest in meaning to the word or phrase in italics

This case is within the <i>jurisdiction</i> of the council
   A) record
   B) prohibition
   C) authority
   D) finance
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: <i>Jurisdiction</i>' the italicized word in the question means the official power to make legal decision and judgement while authority, the answer, is synonymous in that it means the power or right to give orders, make decisions*

**4.** Choose the word(s) or phrase(s) which best fill(s) the gap. If it had rained, we would have had to postpone the concert. It didn't rain, so ____________

   B) we would have had to
   C) we wouldn't have to
   D) we didn't have to
   **Answer: D**
   *Explanation: The conditional statement ended with the first sentence. The following statement should therefore end with a past tense*
