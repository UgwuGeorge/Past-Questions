   A) 3%
   B) 2%
   C) 5%
   D) 4%

where: P denoted principal = N400
T denotes time = 3 years
R denotes interest rate = ?

24 = 400×3×R/100

24 x 100 = 400 x 3 x R

R = (24 × 100)/(400 × 3)

**9.** Find the value of a if the line 2y - ax + 4 = 0 is perpendicular to the line y + (x/4) - 7 = 0
   A) -4
   B) 4
   C) 8
   D) -8
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: 2y - ax + 4 = 0 ⟹
 2y = ax - 4.

y + (x/4) - 7 = 0 ⟹
 y = -x/4 + 7

 Slope of line 1 = a/2

Slope of line 2 = -1/4

-1/4 x a/2 = -1 (product of the slope of two perpendicular lines = -1)

a/8 = 1 ⟹
 a = 8.*
