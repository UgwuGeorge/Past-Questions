# WAEC Economics Past Questions (2012)

## Objectives

**1.** If there arc no barriers to entering a market, it means that
   A) anyone can become a buyer or seller
   B) unwanted goods can always enter the market
   C) the market becomes a dumping ground
   D) the goods are not inspected
   **Answer: A**

**2.** One good reason for the elimination of middlemen is that they
   A) cause increase in price
   B) help in price stability
   C) grade and blend goods
   D) are too many
   **Answer: A**

**3.** A firm will shut down in the long run if its earning is
   A) less than normal profit
   B) greater than normal profit
   C) equal to super normal profit
   D) less than super normal profit
   **Answer: A**

**4.** The co-efficient of income elasticity of demand for inferior goods is
   A) Positive
   B) equal to one
   C) less than one
   D) negative
   **Answer: D**

**5.** Which of the following is not true of small companies?
   A) The cannot benefit from economics of scale
   B) are a good source of new jobs
   C) can satisfy demand in specialist markets
   D) have a good record of technical innovations
   **Answer: D**

**6.** A rational consumer is one who
   A) spends his income to maximize satisfaction
   B) is not influenced by advertisement
   C) behaves in a particular way all the time
   D) knows the prices of all goods and buys the cheapest
   **Answer: A**

**7.** The production strategy used in an over populated country is
   A) import substitution
   B) capital intensive
   C) labour intensive
   D) first come first employed
   **Answer: C**
