# WAEC Economics Past Questions (2006)

## Objectives

**1.** The main difference between a private and a public 
	enterprise is the
   A) amount of profit realized
   B) mode of operation
   C) objective of the business
   D) ownership structure
   **Answer: D**

**2.** If the fixed cost of a firm is 800 Naira and its variable cost is 	2,700 Naira while its total output is 100 units, what is the average cost of the firm?
   A) 25 Naira
   B) 35 Naira
   C) 45 Naira
   D) 50 Naira
   **Answer: B**

**3.** Amount proposed for payments of wages and salaries in a budget is regarded as part of
   A) recurrent expenditure
   B) direct investment
   C) recurrent revenue
   D) capital receipt
   **Answer: A**

**4.** The study of Economics enables individuals to
   A) change jobs.
   B) evade taxes.
   C) accumulate huge wealth
   D) make national decision
   **Answer: D**

**5.** Which of the following is an objective of economic integration?
   A) Harmonization of cultural practices
   B) Free movement of persons, goods and services
   C) Payment of high tariffs
   D) Dumping of goods in less developed countries
   **Answer: B**

**6.** One of the functions of the International Monetary Fund (IMF) is to
   A) devalue the currency of member states
   B) reduce drastically the size of deficit financing of member states
   C) give financial assistance to member states that have temporary balance of payment difficulties
   D) the rapid industrialization process in the less developed nations of the world
   **Answer: C**

**7.** A cheque book is issued to customers of a bank operating a
   A) savings account
   B) time deposit account
   C) current account
   D) fixed deposit account
   **Answer: C**

**8.** What are inferior goods? These are goods
   A) that are generally in short supply.
   B) that are no longer in demand
   C) whose demand falls as income increases
   D) whose price falls as demand increases
   **Answer: C**
