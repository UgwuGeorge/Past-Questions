**1.** Which of the following can be described as high tension transmission?
   A) High resistance and low voltage
   B) Low current and high voltage
   C) High current and low voltage
   D) High current and low resistance
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: High tension transmission is characterized by low current and high voltage, which minimizes power losses during long-distance electricity transmission.*

**5.** Which component of fiber-optic connector has a provision of entry for the fibre along with the fixation to connector housing?

   B) cable
   C) ferrule
