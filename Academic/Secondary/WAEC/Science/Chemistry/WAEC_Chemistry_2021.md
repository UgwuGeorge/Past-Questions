# WAEC Chemistry Past Questions (2021)

## Objectives
1. Which of the following halogens is solid at room temperature?
A) Chlorine
B) Bromine
C) Iodine
D) Fluorine
**Answer: C**

2. The type of bond formed between two atoms of the same element is always:
A) ionic
B) electrovalent
C) metallic
D) non-polar covalent
**Answer: D**
