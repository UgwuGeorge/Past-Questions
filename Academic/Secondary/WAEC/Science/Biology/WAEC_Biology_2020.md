# WAEC Biology Past Questions (2020)

## Objectives
1. The part of the mammalian ear responsible for the maintenance of balance is the:
A) cochlea
B) pinna
C) semi-circular canals
D) tympanic membrane
**Answer: C**

2. Which of the following is an example of an ecosystem?
A) A pond
B) A pride of lions
C) A flock of sheep
D) A herd of cattle
**Answer: A**
