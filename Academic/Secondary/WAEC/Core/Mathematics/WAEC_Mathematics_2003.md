**6.** Each of the interior angles of a regular polygon is 140°. Calculate the sum of all the interior angles of the polygon.
   A) 1080°
   B) 1260°
   C) 2160°
   D) 1800°
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Since each interior angle = 140°;

Each exterior angle = 180° - 140° = 40°

Number of sides of the polygon = 360°/40°
= 9 

Sum of angles in the polygon = 140° x 9

= 1260°*
