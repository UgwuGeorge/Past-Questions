**10.** What is the general term of the sequence 3, 8, 13, 18, ...?
   A) 5n - 2
   B) 5n + 2
   C) 5
   D) 5n
   **Answer: A**
   *Explanation: Given the sequence 3, 8, 13, 18, ... which is an arithmetic sequence

a = 3

d = T<sub>2</sub>  - T<sub>1</sub>  = 8 - 3 = 5

The general term of an A.P is:

T<sub>n</sub>  = a + (n - 1)d

⇒ T<sub>n</sub> = 3 + (n - 1)5

= T<sub>n</sub> = 3 + 5n - 5

∴ T<sub>n</sub> = 5n - 2*
