**1.** The sum of the ages of Musa and Lawal is 28 years. After sharing a certain sum of money in the ratio of their ages, Musa gets N600 and Lawal N800. How old is Lawal?

   B) 20 years
   C) 12 years

M : L = 600 : 800

= 3 : 4

7L/4 = 28

L = 4×28/7


= 16*
