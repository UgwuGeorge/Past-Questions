**9.** The time taken to a piece of work is inversely proportional to the number of men employed. If it takes 45 men to do a piece of work in 5 days, how long will it take 25 men?
   A) 15 days
   B) 12 days
   C) 5days
   D) 9days
   **Answer: D**
   *Explanation: Let t be the time taken Let m the number of men  t ∝1/m t = k/m (k = proportionality constant) when m =45, t=5.    5=k/45.   k =225 When we have 25 men, the number of days will be  t = k/m =225/25  t = 9days*
