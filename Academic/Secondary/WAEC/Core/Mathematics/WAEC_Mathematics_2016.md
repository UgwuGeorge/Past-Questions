**1.** The probability of a student passing any examination is 2/3. If the students takes three examination, what is the probability that he will not pass any of them?
   A) 2/3
   B) 4/9
   C) 8/27
   D) 1/27
   **Answer: D**
   *Explanation: P(pass) = 2/3
P(not pass) = 1 - 2/3 = 1/3
P(not passing any of the 3 Exams) = 1/3 x 1/3 x 1/3
= 1/27*
