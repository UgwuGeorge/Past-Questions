**1.** If P varies inversely as the square root of q, where p = 3 and q = 16, find the value of q when p = 4.
   A) 12
   B) 8
   C) 9
   D) 16
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: p ∝ 1/√q
 
⟹ p = k √q
when p = 3, q = 16.

3=k√16
k = 3 × 4  = 12
∴ p = 12 /√q
when p = 4,

4=12/√q ⟹ √q = 12/4
√q = 3 ⟹ q = 3<sup>2</sup>
q = 9*
