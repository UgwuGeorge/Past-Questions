# WAEC English Language Past Questions (2022)

## Objectives (Lexis and Structure)
1. Choose the word most nearly opposite in meaning to the underlined word:
The manager's completely *unpredictable* behavior made it difficult to work with him.
A) erratic
B) reliable
C) spontaneous
D) dangerous
**Answer: B**

2. Choose the option that best completes the sentence:
If he had studied harder, he ______ the examination.
A) would pass
B) will have passed
C) would passing
D) would have passed
**Answer: D**
