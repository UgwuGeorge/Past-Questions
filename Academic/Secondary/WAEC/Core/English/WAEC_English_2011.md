**4.** The events of last Friday show that there is no love lost between the principal and the Vice-Principal?
   A) They like each other
   B) They work independently
   C) They cannot part company
   D) They dislike each other
   **Answer: D**
   *Explanation: No love lost between two people means that they are not friends, they dislike each other intensely.

If there is "no love lost" between two people, they have a strong hatred, hostility, or enmity for the other person, and make no attempt to conceal it.*
