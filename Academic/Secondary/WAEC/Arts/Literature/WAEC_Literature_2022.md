# WAEC Literature-in-English Past Questions (2022)

## Objectives (General Knowledge of Literature)
1. A poem of fourteen lines is called a:
A) sonnet
B) ballad
C) lyric
D) ode
**Answer: A**

2. The use of consecutive words starting with the same consonant sound is called:
A) assonance
B) pun
C) alliteration
D) simile
**Answer: C**
