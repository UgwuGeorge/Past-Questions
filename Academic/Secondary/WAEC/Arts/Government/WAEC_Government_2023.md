# WAEC Government Past Questions (2023)

## Objectives
1. One of the major disadvantages of a rigid constitution is that it:
   A) is easy to amend
   B) is too voluminous
   C) delays the passage of laws
   D) is difficult to amend in times of emergency
   **Answer: D**

2. The application of the rule of law can be hindered by:
   A) independence of the judiciary
   B) free press
   C) state of emergency
   D) fundamental human rights
   **Answer: C**

## Essay/Theory
1. State five features of a federal system of government.
2. Highlight five ways by which the independence of the judiciary can be guaranteed.
