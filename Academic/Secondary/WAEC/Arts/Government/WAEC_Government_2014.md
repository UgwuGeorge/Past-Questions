**1.** A set of internalized norms which guides political action is called ________
   A) power

   C) law
   D) symbol
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: Laws are formal rules established by a governing authority that are expected to be followed by members of society. They guide behaviour and political action by providing a framework for acceptable conduct and legal processes.*
