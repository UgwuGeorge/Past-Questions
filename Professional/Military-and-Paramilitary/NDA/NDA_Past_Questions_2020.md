# Nigerian Defence Academy (NDA) Past Questions (2020)

## Mathematics
1. Find the quadratic equation whose roots are 2 and -3.
A) x² - x - 6 = 0
B) x² + x - 6 = 0
C) x² - x + 6 = 0
D) x² + x + 6 = 0
**Answer: B**

## General Ability Test
1. Which of the following is the highest rank in the Nigerian Army?
A) Lieutenant General
B) Major General
C) Field Marshal
D) General
**Answer: C** (Note: Field Marshal is the highest, though rarely held. General is the highest practically attained rank).

2. The synonym for 'AUDACIOUS' is:
A) Cowardly
B) Bolder
C) Brilliant
D) Timid
**Answer: B**
