# POLAC Past Questions (2018)

## Questions

**1.** Which of the following formalised colonial penetration into Africa?  
   A) Paris Treaty  
   B) Berlin Conference  
   C) Lagos Treaty  
   D) Cape Coast 
   **Answer: B**

**2.** The colonial government whose constitution introduced elective principle into Nigerian politics was  
   A) Arthur Richards  
   B) Fredrick Lugard  
   C) Hugh Clifford  
   D) John Macpherson 
   **Answer: C**

**3.** Islam was introduced in Northern Nigeria  
   A) before the 13th century  
   B) in the 17th century  
   C) in the 19th century  
   D) in the 18th century 
   **Answer: A**

**4.** Geographically, Nigeria lies between latitudes  
   A) 40 and 140  
   B) 4 and 100   
   C) 140 and 4  
   D) all of the above 
   **Answer: A**

**5.** Pre-colonial Igbo society was  
   A) centralised 
   B) acephalous
   C) feudal  
   D) capitalist 
   **Answer: B**

**6.** The Governor of Anambra State 2014 is  
   A) Mr. Peter Obi 
   B) Owelle Rochas Okorocha 
   C) Peter Odili 
   D) Chris Ngige 
   **Answer: A**

**7.** Alau Dam and Alan Lake are located in  
   A) Calabar 
   B) Okitipupa
   C) Maiduguri 
   D) Warri 
   **Answer: C**

**8.** Gombe State belongs to the ____ geo-political zone in Nigeria 
   A) North Central 
   B) North East 
   C) North West 
   D) South West 
   **Answer: B**

**9.** Ibeno Beach is in  
   A) June 12 1993 
   B) June 14 1993 
   C) June 18 1993 
   D) June 23 1993 
   **Answer: D**
