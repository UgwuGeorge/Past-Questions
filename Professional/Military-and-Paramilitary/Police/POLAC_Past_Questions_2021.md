# POLAC Past Questions (2021)

## Questions

**1.** The introduction of indirect rule in eastern Nigeria led to the Aba Women Riots of  
   A) 1914
   B) 1929
   C) 1935
   D) 1916
   **Answer: B**

**2.** Galadima in the emirate refers to  
   A) a village head  
   B) the office in charge of the army  
   C) the administrator of the capital city  
   D) a judge 
   **Answer: C**

**3.** Which of the following titles among the Igbos in the pre-colonial era required substantial wealth before one could acquire it?
   A) Obi  
   B) Eze  
   C) Ozo  
   D) Ofo 
   **Answer: C**

**4.** The Lagos Colony and the Southern Protectorate of Nigeria were joined in the year 
   A) 1904
   B) 1906
   C) 1910
   D) 1914
   **Answer: B**

**5.** In the Hausa/Fulani traditional political system jingali was  
   A) one of the sources of revenue to cattle owners  
   B) tax paid on cattle  
   C) a property tax paid to land owners 
   D) a type of flat rate tax 
   **Answer: B**

**6.** Gombe State belongs to the ____ geo-political zone in Nigeria 
   A) North Central 
   B) North East 
   C) North West 
   D) South West 
   **Answer: B**

**7.** Nelson Mandela was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 
   A) 1993
   B) 1994
   C) 1995
   D) 1996
   **Answer: A**

**8.** The Minster of Health 2014 is  
   A) Prof.  Olikoye Ransome Kuti 
   B) Mr Muhammed Katsina 
   C) Prof.  C.O.Onyebuchi 
   D) Prof. Muhammed Katsina 
   **Answer: C**

**9.** The capital of Greece is  
   A) Greek 
   B) Athens 
   C) Rome 
   D) Jerusalem 
   **Answer: B**

**10.** The Inspector-General of Police 2014 is 
   A) Mr Iyami Aganike 
   B) Alhaji Mohammed Dahiru Abubakar 
   C) Mr Tafa Balogun 
   D) Mr Sunday Ehindero 
   **Answer: B**
