# Nigeria Police Academy (POLAC) Past Questions (2019)

## General Paper
1. The headquarters of the Nigeria Police Force is located in:
   A) Lagos
   B) Abuja
   C) Kano
   D) Kaduna
   **Answer: B**

2. Who is the current Inspector General of Police? (As of 2019 exam date)
   A) Solomon Arase
   B) Ibrahim Idris
   C) Mohammed Adamu
   D) Usman Alkali Baba
   **Answer: C**
