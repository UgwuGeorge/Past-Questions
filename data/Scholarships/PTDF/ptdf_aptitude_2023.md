# PTDF MSc Aptitude Test (2023)
Detailed study of engineering, sciences, and management questions for Petroleum Technology Development Fund scholarship.

**1.** Which of the following is a major challenge in the development of sustainable energy in Nigeria?
   A) Lack of sunlight
   B) High initial cost of infrastructure
   C) Excessively low population
   D) Abundance of coal
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Renewable energy projects require significant upfront investment, which is currently a bottleneck for mass adoption.*

**2.** In the petroleum industry, 'Upstream' activities primarily involve:
   A) Refining and transport
   B) Distribution and sales
   C) Exploration and production
   D) Retail and marketing
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: Upstream refers to the initial stages of petroleum production, including finding and extracting crude oil.*

**3.** What is the primary role of OPEC?
   A) To regulate global internet traffic
   B) To coordinate petroleum policies of member countries
   C) To provide free medical care to oil workers
   D) To build skyscrapers in member states
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: OPEC (Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries) aims to stabilize oil markets and ensure fair returns.*

**4.** Which gas is the main component of natural gas?
   A) Oxygen
   B) Methane
   C) Nitrogen
   D) CO2
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Methane (CH4) typically makes up about 70-90% of natural gas pipelines.*

**5.** Sustainable development satisfies the needs of the present without:
   A) Increasing the price of oil
   B) Competing with neighboring countries
   C) Compromising the ability of future generations to meet their needs
   D) Reducing the use of plastics
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: This is the Brundtland Commission's definition of sustainable development.*
