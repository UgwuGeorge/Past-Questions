# TotalEnergies Undergraduate Scholarship - Aptitude Test Pattern

## Questions

**1.** TotalEnergies is headquartered in which country?
   A) USA
   B) UK
   C) France
   D) Netherlands
   **Answer: C**

**2.** Which of the following best describes TotalEnergies' strategic direction?
   A) Focus only on oil downstream
   B) Transition to multi-energy: oil, gas, renewables
   C) Divest from all fossil fuels immediately
   D) Expand coal production
   **Answer: B**
