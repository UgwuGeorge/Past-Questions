# Shell Undergraduate Scholarship - Aptitude Test Pattern

## Questions

**1.** Which of the following is a primary objective of the Shell SPDC Scholarship scheme?
   A) To promote healthcare
   B) To support academic excellence in Nigeria
   C) To build roads
   D) To provide electricity
   **Answer: B**

**2.** Shell's core business activity in Nigeria is best described as:
   A) Agriculture
   B) Telecommunications
   C) Exploration and production of oil and gas
   D) Banking
   **Answer: C**

**3.** The Shell Petroleum Development Company (SPDC) is a joint venture between Shell and:
   A) Chevron and ExxonMobil
   B) NNPC, Total, and Agip
   C) Dangote Group
   D) Federal Government of Nigeria
   **Answer: B**
