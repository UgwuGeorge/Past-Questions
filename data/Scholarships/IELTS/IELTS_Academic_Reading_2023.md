# IELTS Academic Reading Sample 2023

## Questions

**1.** According to the passage, what is the primary cause of urban sprawl?
   A) Increased rainfall
   B) Suburbanization
   C) Industrial decline
   D) Afforestation
   **Answer: B**

**2.** The author suggests that future cities should focus on:
   A) Sustainability
   B) Expansion
   C) De-urbanization
   D) Isolation
   **Answer: A**
