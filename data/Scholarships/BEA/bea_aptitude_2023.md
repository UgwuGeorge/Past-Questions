# BEA Scholarship (Bilateral Education Agreement) - Aptitude (2023)
For undergraduate and postgraduate studies abroad. Focus on International Knowledge and Aptitude.

**1.** Which of the following countries is NOT a member of the Bilateral Education Agreement (BEA) with Nigeria?
   A) Russia
   B) China
   C) Morocco
   D) Australia
   **Answer: D**
   *Explanation: Australia is not traditionally part of the BEA partnership, which primarily includes Russia, China, Morocco, Hungary, etc.*

**2.** Who is the current Minister of Education in Nigeria? (General Knowledge calibration)
   A) Mallam Adamu Adamu
   B) Prof. Tahir Mamman
   C) Nyesom Wike
   D) Babatunde Fashola
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Prof. Tahir Mamman was appointed as the Minister of Education in 2023.*

**3.** In the context of the Common Entrance Exam for BEA, what is the primary focus of the 'English' section?
   A) Spelling complex words
   B) Reading comprehension and vocabulary in context
   C) Reciting British poetry
   D) Identifying ancient Latin roots
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Like most standardized tests, the focus is on functional literacy and understanding.*

**4.** Which city is the capital of Morocco?
   A) Casablanca
   B) Marrakesh
   C) Rabat
   D) Tangier
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: Rabat is the administrative and diplomatic center of Morocco.*

**5.** The term 'Bilateral' in BEA refers to:
   A) Something happening twice a year
   B) An agreement between two countries
   C) A focus on science and arts only
   D) A private-public partnership
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Bilateral agreements are made between two sovereign states.*
