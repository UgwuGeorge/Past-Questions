# Commonwealth Master's Scholarship (2024 Edition)
Focus on High-Value Leadership and Academic Excellence.

**1.** The Commonwealth Scholarship program is primarily funded by:
   A) The United Nations
   B) The UK Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office (FCDO)
   C) The African Union
   D) The European Union
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: The FCDO manages the funding for most Commonwealth scholarships.*

**2.** A candidate's 'Personal Statement' should primarily emphasize:
   A) Their personal financial status
   B) Their potential impact on their home country's development
   C) Their favorite hobbies and extracurricular activities
   D) Their list of childhood friends
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Commonwealth scholarships are developmental, so identifying how the candidate will serve their country is key.*

**3.** According to the Commonwealth Charter, 'Democratic Governance' includes:
   A) The use of absolute monarchies for efficiency
   B) Transparency, accountability, and the rule of law
   C) Total censorship of media for social stability
   D) Isolation from international trade
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: The Charter establishes values of openness and accountability as core commonwealth principles.*

**4.** Which of the following is a primary 'Sustainable Development Goal' (SDG) focus for Commonwealth countries?
   A) Mars exploration
   B) Climate Action (SDG 13)
   C) Building high-speed railways between all cities
   D) Reducing the world's population
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Climate change is a critical shared issue for many Commonwealth member states, especially small island nations.*

**5.** The 'Head of the Commonwealth' is currently:
   A) The Secretary-General of the UN
   B) King Charles III
   C) The Prime Minister of India
   D) The President of Nigeria
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: King Charles III succeeded Queen Elizabeth II as Head of the Commonwealth.*
