# NDA Past Questions (2021)

## Questions

**1.** During the early colonial period in Nigeria, the governor ruled the protectorate by  
   A) Crown Colony Orders  
   B) Proclamation  
   C) Order?in-Council  
   D) The Act of Parliament 
   **Answer: D**

**2.** Which of the following formalised colonial penetration into Africa?  
   A) Paris Treaty  
   B) Berlin Conference  
   C) Lagos Treaty  
   D) Cape Coast 
   **Answer: B**

**3.** In the Oyo traditional political system, the Alaafin of Oyo was elected or chosen by a group known 
   A) Ogbon
   B) Oyo Mesi 
   C) Baale
   D) Ohanaeze
   **Answer: B**

**4.** An acephalous pre-colonial system is best represented by the  
   A) Oyo Empire  
   B) Igbo political organisation  
   C) Ijaw political organisation  
   D) Benin Empire 
   **Answer: B**

**5.** The use of forced labour was one of the features of  
   A) British colonial policy  
   B) French colonial policy  
   C) American colonial policy  
   D) divide and rule 
   **Answer: B**

**6.** Abayon, Adim and Adun are ethnic groups in  
   A) Adamawa Stat 
   B) Rivers State 
   C) Bauchi State 
   D) Cross River State 
   **Answer: D**

**7.** ____ was the Chief Justice of Nigeria between 1995 and 2006 
   A) Justice Idris Lagbo Kutigi 
   B) Justice Mohammad Lawal Uwais 
   C) Justice George Sodeinde Sowemimo 
   D) Justice Mohammed Bello 
   **Answer: B**

**8.** Alau Dam and Alan Lake are located in  
   A) Calabar 
   B) Okitipupa
   C) Maiduguri 
   D) Warri 
   **Answer: C**

**9.** Paparada Square is in 
   A) Ilorin 
   B) Lokoja 
   C) Kaduna 
   D) Sokoto 
   **Answer: B**

**10.** Victoria is the capital city of 
   A) Somalia 
   B) Morocco 
   C) Seychelles 
   D) Lesotho 
   **Answer: C**
