# History (2023)

**1.** Nigeria amalgamated its Northern and Southern protectorates in what year?
   A) 1914
   B) 1960
   C) 1963
   D) 1911
   **Answer: A**

**2.** The first modern political party in Nigeria was the
   A) NCNC
   B) NPC
   C) AG
   D) NNDP
   **Answer: D**

**3.** The trans-Atlantic slave trade was officially abolished in the British Empire in
   A) 1807
   B) 1833
   C) 1885
   D) 1900
   **Answer: A**
