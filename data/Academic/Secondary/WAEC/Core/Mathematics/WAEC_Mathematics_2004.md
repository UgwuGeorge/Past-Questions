**7.** The extension of a stretched string is directly proportional to its tension. If the extension produced by a tension of 8 Newton’s is 2cm, find the extension produced by a tension of 12 newton’s.
   A) 2
   B) 1
   C) 0
   D) 3
   **Answer: D**
   *Explanation: Let the extension be E and the tension be T.

Then E∝T
E=kT
when T = 8N, E = 2cm

2=k × 8
k = 2/8 = 0.25
∴ E = 0.25T
when T = 12N, E = 0.25 × 12 =3 cm*
   *(Note: Closest available year from ALOC database: 2015)*

**8.** The sum of the interior angles of a polygon is 20 right angles. How many sides does the polygon have?
   A) 12
   B) 20
   C) 40
   D) 10
   **Answer: A**
   *Explanation: The formula for the sum of the interior angles of a regular polygon = (2n - 4) x 90°

Given: Sum = 20 right angles

(2n - 4) x 90° = 20 times 90°

⇒ 2n - 4 = 20

2n = 24; n = 12.*
   *(Note: Closest available year from ALOC database: 2002)*
