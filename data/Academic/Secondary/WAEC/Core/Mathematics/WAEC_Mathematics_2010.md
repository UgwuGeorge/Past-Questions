**3.** Find the average of the first four prime numbers greater than 10
   A) 20
   B) 19
   C) 17
   D) 15
   **Answer: D**
   *Explanation: Prime numbers are numbers that has only two factors (i.e 1 and itself). They are numbers that are only divisible by 1 and their selves. First four Prime numbers greater than 10 are 11, 13, 17 and 19

  Average = sum of numbers / number

  = (11+13+17+19)4
  = 60/4
  = 15*
   *(Note: Closest available year from ALOC database: 2018)*

**4.** Find the area bounded by the curves y = 4 - x<sup>2</sup> and y = 2x + 1
   A) 20(1/3) sq. units
   B) 20(2/3) sq. units
   C) 10(2/3) sq. units
   D) 10(1/3) sq. units
   **Answer: D**
   *Explanation: Hint:
y = 4 - x<sup>2</sup> and y = 2x + 1
=> 4 - x<sup>2</sup> = 2x + 1
=> x<sup>2</sup> + 2x - 3 = 0
(x+3)(x-1) = 0
thus x = -3 or x = 1.

Integrating x<sup>2</sup> + 2x - 3 from (-3, to 1) w.r.t x will give 31/3 = 10(1/3)*
   *(Note: Closest available year from ALOC database: 2001)*

**7.** If p varies inversely as the square of q and p=8 when q=4, find q when p =32
   A) ± 16
   B) ± 8
   C) ± 4
   D) ± 2
   **Answer: D**
   *Explanation: p ∝ 1/q
p = k/q
K = q<sup>2</sup>p= 4<sup>2</sup>(8)
∴p = 128/q
32 = 128/q
q<sup>2</sup> = 128/32
q<sup>2</sup> = 4
q = √4 = ± 2*

**10.** By how much is the mean of 30, 56, 31, 55, 43 and 44 less than the median?
   A) 0.75
   B) 0.50
   C) 0.33
   D) 0.17
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: Mean = 30+56+31+55+43+44/6=259/6 = 43.167 Median = 30,31,43,44,55,56 = 43+44/2 = 87/2 = 43.5                     Median − mean = 43.5 − 43.17 = 0.33*
