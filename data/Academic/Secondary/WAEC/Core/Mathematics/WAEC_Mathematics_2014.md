**1.** A boat sails 8 km north from P to Q and then sails 6 km west from Q to R. Calculate the bearing of R from P. Give your answer to the nearest degree.
   A) 217<sup>o</sup>
   B) 323<sup>o</sup>
   C) 037<sup>o</sup>
   D) 053<sup>o</sup>
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: tan θ = opp/adj = RQ/QP = 6/8
tan θ = 0.75

θ = tan<sup>−1</sup>(0.75) = 36.87°


∴ The bearing of R from P = 360° - 36.87° = 323°
 (to the nearest degree)*
