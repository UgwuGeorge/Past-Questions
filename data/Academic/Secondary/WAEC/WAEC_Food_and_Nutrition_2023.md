# Food and Nutrition (2023)

**1.** Which of the following vitamins prevents scurvy?
   A) Vitamin C
   B) Vitamin A
   C) Vitamin D
   D) Vitamin K
   **Answer: A**

**2.** The transfer of heat through a fluid by molecular movement is called
   A) Conduction
   B) Convection
   C) Radiation
   D) Evaporation
   **Answer: B**

**3.** Which class of food is primarily responsible for tissue repair?
   A) Carbohydrates
   B) Fats
   C) Proteins
   D) Minerals
   **Answer: C**
