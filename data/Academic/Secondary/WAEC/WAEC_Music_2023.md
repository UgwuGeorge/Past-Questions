# Music (2023)

**1.** How many lines make up a standard musical staff?
   A) 5
   B) 4
   C) 6
   D) 8
   **Answer: A**

**2.** The speed at which a piece of music is played is known as
   A) Pitch
   B) Rhythm
   C) Tempo
   D) Timbre
   **Answer: C**

**3.** Which of these is a woodwind instrument?
   A) Trumpet
   B) Flute
   C) Violin
   D) Drum
   **Answer: B**
