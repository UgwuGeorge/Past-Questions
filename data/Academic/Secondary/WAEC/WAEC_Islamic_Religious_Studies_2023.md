# Islamic Religious Studies

**Instructions:** Answer all 50 questions. Each question carries equal marks.

---

**1.** Which of the following Surahs is considered the cornerstone of Islamic doctrine concerning the Oneness of Allah?
   A) Surah Al-Fatihah
   B) Surah Al-Baqarah
   C) Surah Al-Ikhlas
   D) Surah Al-Falaq
**Answer: C**

**2.** The primary message of Surah Al-Ikhlas is to emphasize:
   A) The pillars of Islam
   B) The history of Prophet Muhammad (S.A.W)
   C) The Unity and Oneness of Allah (Tawheed)
   D) The importance of Hajj
**Answer: C**

**3.** In Surah Al-Ikhlas, the attribute 'As-Samad' means:
   A) The Eternal Absolute
   B) The Most Merciful
   C) The Creator
   D) The Sustainer
**Answer: A**

**4.** Hadith 5 of an-Nawawi's collection primarily warns against:
   A) Shirk
   B) Zina
   C) Riba
   D) Bid'ah (Innovation)
**Answer: D**

**5.** The state of sanctity into which a pilgrim enters for Hajj or Umrah is:
   A) Sa'y
   B) Tawaf
   C) Ihram
   D) Talbiyyah
**Answer: C**

**6.** The Goddesses of the Jahiliyyah Arabs exclude:
   A) Al-Lat
   B) As-Samad
   C) Al-Uzza
   D) Al-Manat
**Answer: B**

**7.** A major consequence of Uthman dan Fodio's Jihad on Hausaland in the 19th century was:
   A) The spread of Christianity
   B) The abolition of Shari'ah
   C) The restoration of Habe Kings
   D) The establishment of the Sokoto Caliphate
**Answer: D**

**8.** The phrase "Qul huwa llāhu aḥad" in Surah Al-Ikhlas translates to:
   A) Allah the Eternal Absolute
   B) Say: He, Allah is One
   C) He begets not, nor is He begotten
   D) And there is none like unto Him

**9.** The number of rak'ahs in the Fajr (Subhi) prayer is:
