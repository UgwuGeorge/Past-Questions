# IRS (2023)

**1.** The first pillar of Islam is
   A) Iman (Faith)
   B) Salat (Prayer)
   C) Zakat (Charity)
   D) Hajj (Pilgrimage)
   **Answer: A**

**2.** The chapter of the Quran revealed first to Prophet Muhammad (SAW) is Surah
   A) Al-Fatiha
   B) Al-Baqarah
   C) Al-Alaq
   D) Al-Ikhlas
   **Answer: C**

**3.** The migration of the Prophet from Makkah to Madinah is known as
   A) Miraj
   B) Hijrah
   C) Isra
   D) Umrah
   **Answer: B**
