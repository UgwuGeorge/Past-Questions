# Physical Education (2023)

**1.** Which of the following is a track event?
   A) Sprint
   B) Shot put
   C) High jump
   D) Javelin
   **Answer: A**

**2.** The game of basketball is started with a
   A) Kick-off
   B) Jump ball
   C) Serve
   D) Face-off
   **Answer: B**

**3.** First aid is primarily given to
   A) Cure the patient
   B) Prevent the condition from worsening
   C) Diagnose the injury
   D) Replace medical treatment
   **Answer: B**
