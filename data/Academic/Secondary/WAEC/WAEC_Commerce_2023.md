# Commerce (2023)

**1.** The primary objective of a commercial enterprise is to
   A) Make profit
   B) Provide free services
   C) Promote laws
   D) Collect taxes
   **Answer: A**

**2.** Which of the following documents is used to reply to an inquiry?
   A) Invoice
   B) Quotation
   C) Receipt
   D) Delivery Note
   **Answer: B**

**3.** The main advantage of a sole proprietorship is
   A) Limited liability
   B) Large capital base
   C) Quick decision making
   D) Continuity
   **Answer: C**
