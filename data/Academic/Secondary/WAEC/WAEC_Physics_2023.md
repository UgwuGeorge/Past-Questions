# Physics (2023)

**1.** The instrument used to measure relative humidity is a
   A) Hydrometer
   B) Barometer
   C) Hygrometer
   D) Anemometer
   **Answer: C**

**2.** Which of the following is NOT a fundamental quantity?
   A) Density
   B) Length
   C) Mass
   D) Time
   **Answer: A**

**3.** A body starts from rest and accelerates uniformly at 5 ms⁻². Its velocity after 4 seconds is
   A) 1.25 ms⁻¹
   B) 20 ms⁻¹
   C) 25 ms⁻¹
   D) 100 ms⁻¹
   **Answer: B**

**4.** Which of the following is a second-class lever?
   A) Scissors
   B) Pliers
   C) Wheelbarrow
   D) Sugar tongs
   **Answer: C**

**5.** The unit of Power is equivalent to
   A) kg·m²·s⁻³
   B) kg·m·s⁻²
   C) kg·m²·s⁻²
   D) kg·m·s⁻¹
   **Answer: A**

**6.** Heat transfer by molecular vibration is known as
   A) Convection
   B) Radiation
   C) Conduction
   D) Evaporation
   **Answer: C**

**7.** Which of the following surfaces is a better absorber of radiant heat?
   A) Polished copper
   B) Dull black
   C) White painted
   D) Shiny silver
   **Answer: B**

**8.** The escape velocity V of a rocket from the earth's surface is given by the formula
   A) V = √(GM/R)
   B) V = √(2GM/R)
   C) V = √(GM/R²)
   D) V = 2GM/R

**9.** Ferromagnetic materials are substances which
   A) are easily magnetized
   B) are difficult to magnetize
   C) have low susceptibility
   D) have high susceptibility
   **Answer: D**

**10.** A sound of frequency 512 Hz reaches a listener 100 m away. If the speed of sound in air is 331 m/s, what is its wavelength?
    A) 0.65 m
    B) 1.55 m
    C) 5.12 m
    D) 3.31 m
    **Answer: A**

**11.** An electron moves from an energy level of -8.6 eV to another level of -3.5 eV. Determine the frequency of the absorbed radiation. [h = 6.6 x 10⁻³⁴ Js, 1eV = 1.6 x 10⁻¹⁹ J]
    A) 1.24 x 10¹⁵ Hz
    B) 1.24 x 10¹⁴ Hz
    C) 1.24 x 10¹⁶ Hz

**13.** The ability of the eye to focus objects at different distances is called
    A) Binocular vision
    B) Accommodation

    D) Astigmatism

**14.** In a simple pendulum experiment, what is the effect of increasing the length of the string by a factor of four?
    A) The period doubles

    C) The period quadruples

**15.** A ball P is released from a roof. At the same time, another ball Q is thrown horizontally from the same point. Which of the following is correct?

    B) Q hits the ground before P

**17.** The specific latent heat of vapourization of lead is 8.7 x 10⁵ J/kg. This means that 8.7 x 10⁵ J of thermal energy is required to change
