# Physics (2023)

**1.** Which of the following describes an object moving with uniform velocity?
   A) Its acceleration is constant but not zero
   B) Its acceleration is zero
   C) Its speed is changing
   D) It covers unequal distances in equal times
   **Answer: B**

**2.** A stone of mass 2kg is dropped from a height of 10m. Calculate its kinetic energy just before it hits the ground. [g = 10m/s²]
   A) 20 J
   B) 50 J
   C) 100 J
   D) 200 J
   **Answer: D**

**3.** The S.I unit of specific heat capacity is
   A) J/kg
   B) J/kg°C
   C) J/°C
   D) kg/J°C
   **Answer: B**

**4.** Which of the following radiations is the least penetrating?
   A) Alpha particles
   B) Beta particles
   C) Gamma rays
   D) X-rays
   **Answer: A**

**5.** An alternating current given by I = 10 sin(100πt) is passed through a resistor. Calculate the peak current.
   A) 100π A
   B) 10 A
   C) 5 A
   D) √10 A
   **Answer: B**

**6.** Total internal reflection occurs only when light passes from
   A) A less dense to a denser medium
   B) A denser to a less dense medium
   C) Any transparent medium to vacuum
   D) A fluid into air
   **Answer: B**

**7.** In a simple pendulum experiment, what is the effect of increasing the length of the string by a factor of four?
   A) The period doubles
   B) The period halves
   C) The period quadruples
   D) The period remains unchanged
   **Answer: A**

**8.** Which of the following properties of waves is not exhibited by sound waves?
   A) Diffraction
   B) Refraction
   C) Polarization
   D) Interference
   **Answer: C**

**9.** A transformer has 500 turns in the primary coil and 100 turns in the secondary coil. If a primary voltage of 250V is applied, what is the secondary voltage?
   A) 1250 V
   B) 500 V
   C) 50 V
   D) 10 V
   **Answer: C**

**10.** Which subatomic particle was discovered by J.J Thomson?
   A) Proton
   B) Neutron
   C) Electron
   D) Positron
   **Answer: C**
