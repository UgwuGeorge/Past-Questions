# Home Management (2023)

**1.** The process of planning family goals and using resources to achieve them is
   A) Home management
   B) Interior decoration
   C) Dietetics
   D) Housekeeping
   **Answer: A**

**2.** A tool used for measuring the body temperature is a
   A) Barometer
   B) Thermometer
   C) Hygrometer
   D) Sphygmomanometer
   **Answer: B**

**3.** Which of the following is an example of a human resource in the home?
   A) Money
   B) Time
   C) Energy
   D) Furniture
   **Answer: C**
