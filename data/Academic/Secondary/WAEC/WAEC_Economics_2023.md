# Economics (2023)

**1.** The opportunity cost of a chosen item is
   A) The value of the next best alternative forgone
   B) The actual price paid
   C) The total cost of production
   D) The consumer surplus
   **Answer: A**

**2.** Inflation is generally described as
   A) A persistent rise in the general price level
   B) An increase in purchasing power
   C) A fall in prices
   D) High employment
   **Answer: A**

**3.** A mixed economy is characterized by the co-existence of
   A) Monopolies and monopsonies
   B) Private and public sectors
   C) Agricultural and industrial sectors
   D) Foreign and local investors
   **Answer: B**
