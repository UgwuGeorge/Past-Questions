# Business Management (2023)

**1.** The span of control refers to
   A) The number of subordinates a manager can effectively supervise
   B) The levels of management
   C) The hierarchy of authority
   D) The physical size of the office
   **Answer: A**

**2.** Which management function involves measuring performance against goals?
   A) Planning
   B) Organizing
   C) Directing
   D) Controlling
   **Answer: D**

**3.** The principle of 'Unity of Command' suggests that
   A) A subordinate should receive orders from one superior only
   B) All commands should be given at once
   C) Unity is power
   D) Managers should command respect
   **Answer: A**
