# Geography (2023)

**1.** Study the map of Abada district and use it to answer questions 1 to 10. The scale of the map in representative fraction is?
   A) 1:200,000
   B) 2:50,000
   C) 1:50,000

**2.** The major occupation of the Bondi settlement is?
   A) Fishing
