# Geography (2023)

**1.** An instrument used to measure atmospheric pressure is the
   A) Barometer
   B) Thermometer
   C) Anemometer
   D) Hygrometer
   **Answer: A**

**2.** The longest river in Africa is the
   A) River Niger
   B) River Nile
   C) River Congo
   D) Zambezi River
   **Answer: B**

**3.** Lines drawn on a map to connect points of equal elevation are called
   A) Contours
   B) Latitudes
   C) Isotherms
   D) Isobars
   **Answer: A**
