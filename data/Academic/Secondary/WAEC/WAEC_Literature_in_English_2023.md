# Literature in English (2023)

**1.** The central idea of a literary work is its
   A) Theme
   B) Plot
   C) Setting
   D) Character
   **Answer: A**

**2.** A figure of speech that gives human qualities to inanimate objects is called
   A) Simile
   B) Metaphor
   C) Personification
   D) Hyperbole
   **Answer: C**

**3.** The time and place of action in a story is referred to as
   A) Climax
   B) Conflict
   C) Setting
   D) Resolution
   **Answer: C**
