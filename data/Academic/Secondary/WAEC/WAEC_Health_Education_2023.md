# WAEC Health Education 2023

1. Which of the following is a symptom of a deficiency in Vitamin A?
A) Scurvy
B) Rickets
C) Beriberi
D) Night blindness
Answer: D

2. The main purpose of the first aid given to an accident victim is to
A) cure the victim
B) prevent the condition from worsening
C) save the victim's life
D) take the victim to the hospital
Answer: C

3. Which of the following is a communicable disease?
A) Diabetes
B) Hypertension
C) Cancer
D) Tuberculosis
Answer: D

4. The most effective way to prevent the spread of malaria is to
A) use mosquito nets
B) take antimalarial drugs
C) clear bushes and stagnant water
D) use insecticides
Answer: A

5. The process of maintaining physical, mental and social well-being is
A) sanitation
B) hygiene
C) healthcare
D) health
Answer: D

6. Which organ filters waste from the blood to form urine?
A) Heart
B) Lungs
C) Kidney
D) Liver
Answer: C

7. An example of a sexually transmitted infection (STI) is
A) Malaria
B) Syphilis
C) Cholera
D) Typhoid
Answer: B

8. The addictive substance found in tobacco is
A) Caffeine
B) Nicotine
C) Alcohol
D) Heroin
Answer: B

9. Which of the following is a balanced diet?

B) A meal with all essential nutrients in right proportions
C) A meal with only proteins
D) A meal with no fats
Answer: B

10. The immunity acquired through vaccination is
A) Natural immunity
B) Artificial active immunity

D) Herd immunity

A) Carbon dioxide
