# Government (2023)

**1.** A system of government where power is divided between a central authority and constituent units is called
   A) Unitary
   B) Federal
   C) Confederal
   D) Monarchy
   **Answer: B**

**2.** The primary function of the legislature is to
   A) Interpret laws
   B) Make laws
   C) Enforce laws
   D) Punish offenders
   **Answer: B**

**3.** A constitution that is rigid is
   A) Easy to amend
   B) Written in stone
   C) Difficult to amend
   D) Unwritten
   **Answer: C**
