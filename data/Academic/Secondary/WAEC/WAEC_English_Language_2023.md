# English Language (2023)

**1.** From the options lettered A to D, choose the word or group of words that is nearest in meaning to the underlined word.

The manager 'reprimanded' his employee for being late.
   A) praised
   B) scolded
   C) encouraged
   D) promoted
   **Answer: B**

**2.** Choose the word nearly opposite in meaning to the italicized word.

The principal gave an *exhaustive* account of the school's finances.
   A) brief
   B) detailed
   C) boring
   D) accurate
   **Answer: A**

**3.** Choose the option that best completes the sentence.

The players ____ disappointed when they lost the match.
   A) was
   B) were
   C) is
   D) has been
   **Answer: B**

**4.** The politician was accused of embezzling public funds; he was asked to ____ by his constituents.
   A) step down
   B) step up
   C) step across
   D) step over
   **Answer: A**

**5.** Choose the correctly spelled word.
   A) Accomodation
   B) Accommodations
   C) Accommodation
   D) Acommodation
   **Answer: C**

**6.** He preferred reading ____ watching television.
   A) than
   B) to
   C) from
   D) more than
   **Answer: B**

**7.** The jury ____ unable to reach a unanimous decision.
   A) was
   B) were
   C) are
   D) have been
   **Answer: A**

**8.** Identify the word that has the same consonant sound as the underlined letters in: ro'gh'
   A) ghost
   B) graph
   C) though
   D) bough
   **Answer: B**

**9.** If I had known he was coming, I ____ him at the station.
   A) would have met
   B) will meet
   C) met
   D) will have met
   **Answer: A**

**10.** The meeting was adjoured 'sine die'. sine die means?
   A) indefinitely
   B) immediately
   C) at random
   D) without permission
   **Answer: A**
