# Financial Accounting (2023)

**1.** Which of the following is considered an intangible asset?
   A) Goodwill
   B) Motor vehicle
   C) Land
   D) Cash in hand
   **Answer: A**

**2.** A debit balance on a bank statement indicates
   A) An overdraft
   B) A favorable balance
   C) A cash deposit
   D) An error
   **Answer: A**

**3.** What is the primary purpose of a trial balance?
   A) To calculate profit
   B) To test the arithmetical accuracy of ledgers
   C) To show the financial position
   D) To record daily transactions
   **Answer: B**
