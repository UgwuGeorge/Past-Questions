# Biology

**Instructions:** Answer all 50 questions. Each question carries equal marks.

---

**1.** The type of nutrition in which organisms take solid organic materials into their body is:
   A) parasitic
   B) holozoic
   C) saprophytic
   D) symbiotic
**Answer: B**

**2.** The ability of organisms to maintain a constant internal environment is known as:
   A) diuresis
   B) plasmolysis
   C) endosmosis
   D) homeostasis
**Answer: D**

**3.** Organisms that feed on dead, decaying tissues of other organisms are known as:
   A) saprophytes
   B) autotrophs
   C) parasites
   D) heterotrophs
**Answer: A**

**4.** Which of the following activities is against the conservation of natural resources?
   A) Contour ploughing
   B) Establishment of game reserves
   C) Deforestation
   D) Afforestation
**Answer: C**

**5.** A student drew a mango leaf during a practical test and failed. Study of the diagram revealed the student missed some features. Which part of the leaf is primarily responsible for photosynthesis?
   A) Midrib
   B) Lamina
   C) Petiole
   D) Stipule
**Answer: B**

**6.** Which of the following is the causative agent of cholera?
   A) *Plasmodium falciparum*
   B) *Vibrio cholerae*
   C) *Salmonella typhi*
   D) *Mycobacterium tuberculosis*
**Answer: B**

**7.** An organism possesses a soft body, a muscular foot, and a shell. To which phylum does it belong?
   A) Arthropoda
   B) Mollusca
   C) Annelida
   D) Chordata
**Answer: B**

**8.** In a plant cell diagram showing photosynthesis, oxygen gas is formed and diffuses out from which labeled part?
   A) Chloroplast
   B) Mitochondrion
   C) Nucleus
   D) Vacuole
**Answer: A**

**9.** The process by which complex substances are broken down into simpler ones with the release of energy is:
   A) Anabolism
   B) Metabolism
   C) Catabolism
   D) Growth
**Answer: C**

**10.** Which of the following sense organs respond to the stimulus of chemicals?
   A) Eyes
   B) Tongue
   C) Ears
   D) Skin
**Answer: B**

**11.** The lumbar vertebra differs from the thoracic vertebra because it possesses a:
   A) longer neural spine.
   B) wider neural canal.
   C) thicker centrum.
   D) shorter transverse process.

**12.** Which of the following blood vessels carries oxygenated blood into the heart?
   A) Pulmonary artery
   B) Anterior vena cava
   C) Pulmonary vein
   D) Posterior vena cava
**Answer: C**

**13.** Filtrates in the Bowman's capsule contain vitamins because:
   A) they have low molecular weight.
   B) most of them are fat soluble.
   C) they are actively secreted.
   D) the capsule is impermeable to proteins.

**14.** The process of maintaining water and salt balance in the body of an organism is:

**15.** Which part of the brain is responsible for the coordination of muscular movements and balance?

**16.** Which of the following processes does not occur during photosynthesis?
   A) Photolysis of water

   A) Denitrification
   B) Nitrification

   A) Putrefaction
