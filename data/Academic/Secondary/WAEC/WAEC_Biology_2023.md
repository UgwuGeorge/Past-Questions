# Biology (2023)

**1.** In which organelle does cellular respiration primarily take place?
   A) Nucleus
   B) Ribosome
   C) Mitochondrion
   D) Chloroplast
   **Answer: C**

**2.** Which of the following blood vessels carries deoxygenated blood to the lungs?
   A) Aorta
   B) Pulmonary artery
   C) Pulmonary vein
   D) Vena cava
   **Answer: B**

**3.** The end product of protein digestion is
   A) Glucose
   B) Fatty acids
   C) Glycerol
   D) Amino acids
   **Answer: D**

**4.** Which part of the brain controls voluntary actions and memory?
   A) Cerebellum
   B) Medulla oblongata
   C) Cerebrum
   D) Hypothalamus
   **Answer: C**

**5.** A person with blood group O is considered a universal donor because their blood lacks
   A) Antigens A and B
   B) Antibodies a and b
   C) Hemoglobin
   D) Rhesus factor
   **Answer: A**

**6.** The relationship between a tick and a dog is an example of
   A) Mutualism
   B) Commensalism
   C) Parasitism
   D) Predation
   **Answer: C**

**7.** Photosynthesis is crucial to life mostly because it
   A) Releases carbon dioxide into the air
   B) Provides oxygen and organic food
   C) Provides nitrogen for plants
   D) Uses up water resources
   **Answer: B**

**8.** Which vertebrate class is characterized by having fur or hair and mammary glands?
   A) Aves
   B) Amphibia
   C) Reptilia
   D) Mammalia
   **Answer: D**

**9.** The primary function of the root hairs in a plant is to
   A) Anchor the plant to the soil
   B) Protect the growing root tip
   C) Increase the surface area for water absorption
   D) Store starch
   **Answer: C**

**10.** In a monohybrid cross between two heterozygous parents (Tt x Tt), what is the expected phenotypic ratio of the offspring?
   A) 1:1
   B) 1:2:1
   C) 3:1
   D) 9:3:3:1
   **Answer: C**
