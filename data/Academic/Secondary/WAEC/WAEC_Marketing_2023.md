# Marketing (2023)

**1.** The 4Ps of marketing are Product, Price, Place, and
   A) Promotion
   B) Package
   C) People
   D) Process
   **Answer: A**

**2.** Dividing a broad market into subsets of consumers sharing common needs is known as
   A) Market targeting
   B) Market segmentation
   C) Market positioning
   D) Market research
   **Answer: B**

**3.** Which of the following is a direct marketing channel?
   A) Wholesaler
   B) Retailer
   C) Telemarketing
   D) Distributor
   **Answer: C**
