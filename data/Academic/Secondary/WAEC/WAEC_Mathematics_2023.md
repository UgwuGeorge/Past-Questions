# Mathematics (2023)

**1.** Find the value of x if 3x - 5 = 10.
   A) 3
   B) 5
   C) 10
   D) 15
   **Answer: B**

**2.** Simplify (2^3 × 2^5) / 2^4
   A) 2^2
   B) 2^3
   C) 2^4
   D) 2^8
   **Answer: C**

**3.** A man buys an item for ₦4,000 and sells it for ₦5,200. Calculate his percentage profit.
   A) 10%
   B) 20%
   C) 30%
   D) 40%
   **Answer: C**

**4.** If the union of two sets A and B is equal to the intersection of A and B, which of the following is true?
   A) A is empty
   B) B is empty
   C) A is a subset of B
   D) A is equal to B
   **Answer: D**

**5.** Find the roots of the quadratic equation x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0.
   A) 3 and -2
   B) -3 and -2
   C) 2 and 3
   D) -2 and 3
   **Answer: C**

**6.** Solve the inequality: 2(x + 3) > 5(x - 1).
   A) x > 11/3
   B) x < 11/3
   C) x < -11/3
   D) x > -11/3
   **Answer: B**

**7.** What is the mean of the numbers 4, 8, 12, 16, 20?
   A) 10
   B) 12
   C) 14
   D) 16
   **Answer: B**

**9.** If a fair coin is tossed three times, what is the probability of obtaining at least two heads?
   A) 1/8
   B) 3/8
   C) 1/2
   D) 5/8
