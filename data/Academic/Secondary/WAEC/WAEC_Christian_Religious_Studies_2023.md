# WAEC Christian Religious Studies 2023

1. In which of the following ways did God evaluate His work after creation?
A) Very perfect
B) Satisfactory
C) Excellent
D) Very good
Answer: D

2. On the fifth day, the manifestation of the power of God's word was evident in the creation of the
A) light and darkness
B) firmament and dry land
C) sun, moon and stars
D) aquatic animals and flying creatures
Answer: D

3. God decided not to divide the kingdom of Israel during the reign of Solomon because
A) Solomon was very wise
B) Solomon repented of his sins
C) of God's love for David
D) the people remained faithful to God
Answer: C

4. Which of the following people led the rebuilding of the temple in Jerusalem after the Jews returned from exile?
A) Ezra and Nehemiah
B) Zerubbabel and Joshua
C) Haggai and Zechariah
D) Cyrus and Darius
Answer: B

5. The temptations of Jesus in the wilderness were meant to
A) test his magical powers
B) show that there was temptation after baptism
C) prepare Jesus adequately for his ministry
D) show that the devil has great power
Answer: C

6. Which of the following was the reason for which the believers were scattered abroad after the death of Stephen?
A) There was a great famine in Jerusalem
B) There was a great persecution against the church in Jerusalem
C) To fulfill the command to spread the gospel
D) Jesus appeared to them and told them to leave
Answer: B

7. When David was informed that the child born to him by Bathsheba had died, he
A) wept throughout the day
B) tore his robes and went to his house
C) refused to eat or drink for seven days
D) anointed himself and worshipped the Lord
Answer: D

8. Which of the following was the reason for the tension among neighbors in the time of Amos?
A) Religious differences
B) Lack of social justice and oppression of the poor
C) Territorial disputes
D) Disagreements over trade routes
Answer: B

9. According to James, if any one lacks wisdom, he should ask God
A) with fasting and prayer
B) in faith, without doubting
C) while making a sacrifice
D) through the elders of the church
Answer: B

10. The events following the Samaritans' request to build with the Jews showed that
A) the Samaritans were truly helpful
B) there was mutual cooperation between the two groups
C) the Jews were exclusive and rejected foreign help
D) the Samaritans were indifferent to the project
Answer: C

11. God's evaluation of His work after creation in Genesis was that it was
A) perfect
B) very good
C) holy
D) blessed
Answer: B

12. John the Baptist's reluctance to baptize Jesus was because he
A) felt unworthy
B) feared the Jewish leaders
C) did not recognize Jesus
D) thought Jesus was a sinner
Answer: A

13. The reason believers were scattered abroad after the death of Stephen was
A) to escape persecution
B) to obey the Great Commission
C) due to a disagreement among apostles
D) because of a great famine
Answer: A

14. Hosea's marriage to a harlot was intended to teach the Israelites about
A) faithfulness
B) child education
C) agricultural practices
D) trade relations
Answer: A
