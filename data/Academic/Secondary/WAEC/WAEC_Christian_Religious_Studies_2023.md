# CRS (2023)

**1.** Who led the Israelites out of Egypt?
   A) Moses
   B) Abraham
   C) Joshua
   D) David
   **Answer: A**

**2.** The beatitudes were taught by Jesus during the
   A) Sermon on the Mount
   B) Last Supper
   C) Transfiguration
   D) Triumphal Entry
   **Answer: A**

**3.** Paul's conversion took place on the road to
   A) Jerusalem
   B) Damascus
   C) Antioch
   D) Rome
   **Answer: B**
