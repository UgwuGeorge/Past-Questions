# NECO Biology Past Questions (2001)

## Objectives

**1.** Which of the following is not a characteristic of living things?
   A) Growth
   B) Reproduction
   C) Movement
   D) Respiration
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: Not all living things can move from place to place (e.g., plants).*

**2.** The basic structural unit of all living organisms is the
   A) Tissue
   B) Organ
   C) Cell
   D) System
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: The cell is the smallest unit of life.*

**3.** In humans, the respiratory organ is the
   A) Heart
   B) Lungs
   C) Liver
   D) Kidneys
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Lungs are the organs of respiration.*

**4.** The pigment that makes plants green is
   A) Carotene
   B) Chlorophyll
   C) Xanthophyll
   D) Anthocyanin
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Chlorophyll absorbs light for photosynthesis.*

**5.** Which of the following is a function of the kidney?
   A) Production of bile
   B) Filtering blood
   C) Production of insulin
   D) Production of hormones
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Kidneys filter blood to remove waste.*

**6.** The type of blood vessel that returns blood to the heart is the
   A) Artery
   B) Vein
   C) Capillary
   D) Arteriole
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Veins return deoxygenated blood to the heart.*

**7.** In plants, the male reproductive part is the
   A) Stigma
   B) Style
   C) Anther
   D) Ovary
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: The anther produces pollen.*

**8.** Which vitamin is needed for healthy bones and teeth?
   A) Vitamin A
   B) Vitamin B
   C) Vitamin C
   D) Vitamin D
   **Answer: D**
   *Explanation: Vitamin D aids calcium absorption for bones.*

**9.** The process of cell eating is called
   A) Pinocytosis
   B) Phagocytosis
   C) Osmosis
   D) Diffusion
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Phagocytosis is the engulfing of large particles.*

**10.** The hormone that regulates sugar level in blood is
   A) Adrenaline
   B) Insulin
   C) Thyroxine
   D) Cortisol
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Insulin lowers blood glucose levels.*
