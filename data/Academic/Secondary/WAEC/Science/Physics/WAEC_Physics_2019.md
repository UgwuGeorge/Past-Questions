**2.** A charged particle is moving in a uniform magnetic field. if the direction of motion of the charged particle is parallel to the magnetic field, the path of the charge will
   A) Be a straight line
   B) Curve inwards
   C) Be a parabola
   D) Curve outwards
   **Answer: A**
   *Explanation: When a charged particle moves parallel to a magnetic field, it experiences no magnetic force and continues in a straight line.*

**3.** Which of the following has the lowest internal resistance when new?
   A) Leclanche cell
   B) Daniell cell
   C) Torch battery
   D) Accumulator
   **Answer: D**
   *Explanation: The accumulator (lead-acid battery) has the lowest internal resistance when new, which allows it to deliver large currents efficiently. This is why it's used in applications requiring high current, such as starting car engines.*

**6.** The thermometer whose thermometric property is change in volume with temperature is
   A) Resistance thermometer
   B) Thermocouple
   C) Bimetallic thermometer
   D) Liquid-in-glass thermometer
   **Answer: D**
   *Explanation: A liquid-in-glass thermometer operates on the principle that liquids (like mercury or alcohol) expand and contract with temperature changes. This change in volume of the liquid causes it to rise or fall in a calibrated glass tube, allowing temperature measurement.*

**9.** A particle of mass 10⁻² kg is fixed to the tip of a fan blade which rotates with angular velocity of 100 rad s⁻¹. If the radius of the blade is 0.2m, the centripetal force is?
   A) 2N
   B) 20N
   C) 200N
   D) 400N
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Given information:

Mass (m) = 10⁻² kg = 0.01 kg
Angular velocity (ω) = 100 rad/s
Radius (r) = 0.2 m
Centripetal force (F) = ?

Formula for centripetal force:
F = mω²r
Substituting values:
F = 0.01 × (100)² × 0.2
F = 0.01 × 10,000 × 0.2
F = 0.01 × 2,000
F = 20 N*
