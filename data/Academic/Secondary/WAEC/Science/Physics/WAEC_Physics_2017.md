**9.** If a source of solid is moving, a stationary listener will hear a sound of different frequency. This is called?
   A) doppler effect
   B) resonance
   C) ultra sound
   D) rarefaction
   **Answer: A**
   *Explanation: The Doppler effect or Doppler shift is the change in frequency of a wave in relation to an observer who is moving relative to the wave source.

The drop in pitch of ambulance sirens as they pass by and the shift in red light are common examples of the Doppler Effect.*
   *(Note: Closest available year from ALOC database: 2020)*

**10.** When a bus is accelerating, it must be
   A) changing its speed
   B) changing its velocity
   C) changing its position
   D) changing its direction
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: Acceleration is defined as the rate of change of velocity. This means a bus can accelerate by changing its speed, direction, or both. However, the most general definition of acceleration involves a change in velocity.*
