# Fine Arts (2023)

**1.** The primary colors in painting are
   A) Red, Blue, Yellow
   B) Red, Green, Blue
   C) Orange, Violet, Green
   D) Black and White
   **Answer: A**

**2.** A two-dimensional art form created by applying paint to a surface is called
   A) Sculpture
   B) Painting
   C) Ceramics
   D) Textiles
   **Answer: B**

**3.** The famous Nok culture is popular for its
   A) Wood carving
   B) Terracotta sculptures
   C) Bronze casting
   D) Glassmaking
   **Answer: B**
