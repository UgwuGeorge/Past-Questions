# Office Practice (2023)

**1.** Which of these is a core function of an office?
   A) Receiving information
   B) Manufacturing goods
   C) Farming
   D) Mining
   **Answer: A**

**2.** A mail register is used to record
   A) Incoming and outgoing mails
   B) Staff attendance
   C) Financial transactions
   D) Inventory updates
   **Answer: A**

**3.** Which equipment is most suitable for destroying confidential documents?
   A) Photocopier
   B) Shredder
   C) Stapler
   D) Perforator
   **Answer: B**
