**1.** If sin θ = -1/2 for 0 < θ < 360°, the value of θ is
   A) 30° and 150°
   B) 150° and 210°
   C) 210° and 330°
   D) 150° and 330°
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: sin θ = -1/2
= -0.5
θ = sin-1 (0.5)

Since θ is negative
θ = 180 + 30 = 210°
θ = 360 - 30 = 330°
∴θ = 210° and 330°*

**2.** If given two points A(3, 12) and B(5, 22) on a x-y plane. Find the equation of the straight line with intercept at 2.
   A) y = 5x + 2
   B) y = 5x + 3
   C) y = 12x + 2
   D) y = 22x + 3
   **Answer: A**
   *Explanation: The equation of a straight line is given as y=mx+b
 

where m = the slope of the line

b = intercept

Given points A(3, 12) and B(5, 22), the slope = (22−12)/(5−3)= 10/2 = 5

Hence, the equation of the line is y = 5x + 2

**6.** The mean of a set of six numbers is 60. If the mean of the first five is 50, find the sixth number in the set.
   A) 105
   B) 100
   C) 95
   D) 110
   **Answer: D**
   *Explanation: Let the sum of the first five numbers and the sixth number be x and t respectively.

(x+t)/6 = 60 ⟹ x + t = 360
x/5 = 50 ⟹ x = 250
∴ t = 360 − 250 = 110*

m = 0 and n = 4.*
