# NECO Government Past Questions (2013)

## Objectives

**1.** In dividing a country into constituencies, it is important to
   A) ask for permission from the traditional ruler in that area
   B) consider historical
   C) display the voters register
   D) involve the politicians in the areas
   **Answer: B**

**2.** To conduct local government elections, the electoral commission divides the local government area into
   A) branches
   B) constituencies
   C) secretariats
   D) wards.
   **Answer: D**

**3.** The special box prepared by the electoral commission into which voters cast their votes is called
   A) ballot box.
   B) ballot paper.
   C) logo.
   D) polling booth.
   **Answer: A**

**4.** To contest an election, a candidate must be a member of a
   A) campaign organization.
   B) civil society organization.
   C) national assembly
   D) political party.
   **Answer: D**

**5.** Nigeria became a member country of the Common wealth of Nations on 1st October
   A) 1922
   B) 1951
   C) 1960
   D) 1963
   **Answer: C**

**6.** The cultural organisation which metamorphosed into 	Northern People’s Congress (NPC) was
   A) Arewa Consultative Forum
   B) Borno Youth Movement
   C) Egbe Omo Oduduwa
   D) Jamiyyar Mutanen Arewa
   **Answer: D**

**7.** Which of the following is NOT a source of local government finance?
   A) Commercial ventures
   B) Grants
   C) Rates
   D) Sale of bonds
   **Answer: D**

**8.** The Headquarters of Organization of Petroleum Exporting Countries (OPEC) is located in
   A) Algiers
   B) Baghdad
   C) Riyadh
   D) Vienna
   **Answer: D**
