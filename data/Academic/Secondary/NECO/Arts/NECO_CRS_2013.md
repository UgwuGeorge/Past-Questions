# NECO CRS Past Questions (2013)

## Objectives

**1.** David showed Nathan the prophet that he had repented of his sin by
   A) refusing to marry Uriah’s wife.
   B) confessing that he had sinned.
   C) offering to compensate Uriah’s family
   D) making sacrifice to please God.
   **Answer: B**

**2.** When Joseph was sent to his brothers, he met them pasturing at  
   A) Hebron.
   B) Bethel.
   C) Beersheba
   D) Dothan
   **Answer: D**

**3.** According to Luke, when Moses and Elijah appeared during the Transfiguration, they spoke of Jesus’
   A) departure
   B) second coming
   C) resurrection
   D) trials.
   **Answer: A**

**4.** Saul was converted on his journey to  
   A) Samaria
   B) Jerusalem.
   C) Salamis.
   D) Damascus
   **Answer: D**

**5.** “Can any one forbid water for baptizing these people who have received the Holy Spirit just as we have?” The statement above was made by Peter at the conversion of
   A) Herod
   B) Lydia
   C) Saul
   D) Cornelius.
   **Answer: D**

**6.** The gospel which gives prominence to the Holy Spirit in the life of Jesus Christ is
   A) Matthew.
   B) Mark.
   C) John.
   D) Luke.
   **Answer: D**

**7.** True religion, according to Amos’ submission, is the one in which  
   A) God is always appeased with sacrifices and tithes
   B) Foods and drinks are served as a mark of love
   C) Justice and righteousness flows like an endless stream
   D) Feast and solemn assemblies play important roles
   **Answer: C**

**8.** Name three places Christian religious people look up to find the truth about the nature of God
   A) In the sacred scripture
   B) In the religious leader
   C) In the church
   D) In their conscience.
   **Answer: D**

**9.** Joseph named his first son “Manasseh” because God made him 
   A) king in Egypt
   B) forget his hardship.
   C) a ruler of Isreal.
   D) fruitful in his father’s land.
   **Answer: B**
