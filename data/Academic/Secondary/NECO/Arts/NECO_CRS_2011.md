# NECO CRS Past Questions (2011)

## Objectives

**1.** “Tabitha, arise”. These were said by Peter during the 
   A) healing of Aeneas from paralysis.
   B) raising of Dorcas from death.
   C) healing of the lame man.
   D) converting of Simeon the magician
   **Answer: B**

**2.** According to Ezra, the return of the Jews from exile was prophesied by
   A) Ezekiel
   B) Amos
   C) Isaiah
   D) Jeremiah
   **Answer: A**

**3.** Because David sought God’s will to pursue the Amalekites 	who had invaded Ziklag, he was rewarded with victory and
   A) many new territories
   B) spoils
   C) many prisoners of war
   D) favour
   **Answer: B**

**4.** The visitors of Peter after his vision at Joppa were 
   A) Jerusalem
   B) Samaria
   C) Caesarea
   D) Antioch
   **Answer: A**

**5.** Prophet Ezekiel was set upon his feet to hear the LORD speak when
   A) an angel of the LORD lifted him
   B) a voice from heaven commanded him to do so
   C) the Spirit entered into him
   D) He was made to touch the ephod
   **Answer: D**

**6.** According to Peter, Christians who suffer for Christ should not be ashamed but should
   A) glorify God.
   B) be encouraged.
   C) be patient.
   D) be grateful.
   **Answer: B**

**7.** Deborah, a prophetess who was a judge in Israel was the wife of
   A) Joshua.
   B) Abijah.
   C) Lappidoth.
   D) Sisera.
   **Answer: C**

**8.** The tribes which lead the Jews in rebuilding the temple were
   A) Judah and Benjamin
   B) Levi and Gad
   C) Ephraim and Manasseh
   D) Reuben and Zebulum
   **Answer: A**

**9.** Jesus’ baptism was significant because it
   A) initiated him into Judaism
   B) cleansed Him from sin
   C) awakened the messianic spirit in Him
   D) marked the call into the messianic ministry
   **Answer: D**

**10.** According to Paul in Philippians, Jesus demonstrated humility when He
   A) had pity on the possessed man
   B) likened Himself to God
   C) forgave those who crucified Him
   D) took the form of man
   **Answer: D**
