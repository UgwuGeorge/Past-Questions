# WAEC Biology Past Questions - 2023

## Questions

**1.** Which of the following structures is primarily involved in the transport of nutrients in plants?
   P) Phloem
   X) Xylem
   S) Stomata
   R) Root hairs
   **Answer: A**

**2.** During which phase of the cell cycle does DNA replication occur?
   G) G2 phase
   S) S phase
   M) M phase
   **Answer: B**

**3.** Which of the following is a benefit of biodiversity?
   I) Increased susceptibility to diseases
   E) Ecosystem resilience
   M) Monoculture advantages
   R) Reduced genetic variety
   **Answer: B**

**4.** What is the role of ribosomes in a cell?
   E) Energy production
   P) Protein synthesis
   N) Nucleic acid synthesis
   C) Cell division
   **Answer: B**

**5.** In plants, which process occurs primarily in the chloroplasts?
   C) Cell respiration
   P) Photosynthesis
   N) Nitrogen fixation
   T) Transpiration
   **Answer: B**

**6.** The primary function of the large intestine is to:
   A) Absorb water and electrolytes
   D) Digest proteins
   P) Produce bile
   **Answer: B**

**7.** Which part of the plant is primarily responsible for absorbing water and minerals from the soil?
   S) Stem
   L) Leaves
   R) Roots
   F) Flowers
   **Answer: C**

**8.** Which of the following processes is characterized by the movement of molecules from an area of higher concentration to an area of lower concentration?
   O) Osmosis
   D) Diffusion
   A) Active transport
   F) Filtration
   **Answer: B**

**9.** What organelle is responsible for controlling the activities of a cell?
   M) Mitochondria
   N) Nucleus
   E) Endoplasmic reticulum
   G) Golgi apparatus
   **Answer: B**

**10.** Which of the following is NOT a characteristic of living organisms?
   R) Response to stimuli
   G) Growth and development
   A) Ability to reproduce
   U) Unchanging structure
   **Answer: D**

**11.** In the human body, which organ is MOST involved in detoxification of harmful substances?
   K) Kidney
   L) Lung
   H) Heart
   **Answer: B**

**12.** Which type of immunity is provided by vaccines?
   P) Passive immunity
   N) Natural immunity
   A) Active immunity
   C) Cellular immunity
   **Answer: C**

**13.** Which part of the brain is responsible for coordinating voluntary movements?
   C) Cerebellum
   B) Brain stem
   H) Hypothalamus
   **Answer: B**

**14.** In genetics, what term describes an organism's observable traits?
   P) Phenotype
   G) Genotype
   H) Heterozygous
   **Answer: A**

**15.** Which of the following macromolecules serves as an important energy source for living organisms?
   P) Proteins
   L) Lipids
   N) Nucleic acids
   V) Vitamins
   **Answer: B**
