# WAEC Biology Past Questions - 2022

## Questions

**1.** Which of the following structures is responsible for protein synthesis in a cell?
   N) Nucleus
   R) Ribosome
   G) Golgi apparatus
   L) Lysosome
   **Answer: B**

**2.** What is the primary function of the xylem in plants?
   T) Transport of water
   P) Photosynthesis
   S) Storage of carbohydrates
   **Answer: B**

**3.** In which process do organisms break down glucose to release energy without oxygen?
   A) Anaerobic respiration
   P) Photolysis
   F) Fermentation
   **Answer: B**

**4.** Which hormone is primarily responsible for regulating blood sugar levels?
   I) Insulin
   G) Glucagon
   A) Adrenaline
   C) Cortisol
   **Answer: A**

**5.** What is the term for an organism's observable characteristics resulting from the interaction of its genotype with the environment?
   P) Phenotype
   G) Genotype
   A) Allele
   K) Karyotype
   **Answer: A**

**6.** Which type of RNA carries amino acids to the ribosome during protein synthesis?
   M) Messenger RNA (mRNA)
   T) Transfer RNA (tRNA)
   R) Ribosomal RNA (rRNA)
   S) Small nuclear RNA (snRNA)
   **Answer: B**

**7.** In an ecological pyramid, which level would have the greatest biomass?
   P) Primary consumers
   S) Secondary consumers
   T) Tertiary consumers
   **Answer: A**

**8.** What is the process by which certain bacteria convert nitrogen gas from the atmosphere into ammonia?
   N) Nitrogen fixation
   D) Denitrification
   A) Ammonification
   **Answer: B**

**9.** Which part of the human brain is responsible for regulating heart rate and breathing?
   C) Cerebellum
   M) Medulla oblongata
   P) Pons
   **Answer: C**

**10.** Which structure in the plant cell is responsible for maintaining turgor pressure?
   C) Cell wall
   V) Vacuole
   M) Mitochondria
   **Answer: B**

**11.** Which of the following best describes the function of the smooth endoplasmic reticulum?
   P) Protein synthesis
   D) Detoxification and lipid synthesis
   C) Cell division
   R) Ribosome assembly
   **Answer: B**

**12.** Which type of inheritance pattern involves the expression of both alleles in the phenotype?
   C) Codominance
   I) Incomplete dominance
   E) Epistasis
   **Answer: B**

**13.** What is the primary role of the sinoatrial (SA) node in the heart?
   P) Pumping blood
   G) Generating electrical impulses
   R) Regulating blood pressure
   S) Stimulating breathing
   **Answer: B**

**14.** Which mechanism primarily drives the movement of water through plants?
   A) Active transport
   T) Transpiration pull
   D) Diffusion
   C) Capillary action
   **Answer: B**
