# WAEC Biology Past Questions - 2020

## Questions

**1.** Which organelle is responsible for ATP production in eukaryotic cells?
   R) Ribosome
   M) Mitochondrion
   E) Endoplasmic Reticulum
   G) Golgi Apparatus
   **Answer: B**

**2.** What is the function of the large intestine in humans?
   D) Digestion of proteins
   A) Absorption of water
   P) Production of bile
   S) Secretion of insulin
   **Answer: B**

**3.** In which phase of cellular respiration is the majority of ATP produced?
   G) Glycolysis
   K) Krebs Cycle
   E) Electron Transport Chain
   F) Fermentation
   **Answer: C**

**4.** Which group of plants is characterized by having seeds that are not enclosed in an ovary?
   A) Angiosperms
   G) Gymnosperms
   B) Bryophytes
   P) Pteridophytes
   **Answer: B**

**5.** What is the primary role of chlorophyll in photosynthesis?
   A) Absorb light energy
   R) Release carbon dioxide
   **Answer: C**

**6.** Which of the following macromolecules is primarily responsible for the structure and function of enzymes?
   N) Nucleic Acids
   C) Carbohydrates
   P) Proteins
   L) Lipids
   **Answer: C**

**7.** Which process is described as the process by which cells convert glucose into energy in the absence of oxygen?
   A) Anaerobic Respiration
   P) Photosynthesis
   T) Transpiration
   **Answer: B**

**8.** What term is used to describe the movement of water across a selectively permeable membrane?
   D) Diffusion
   O) Osmosis
   A) Active Transport
   F) Facilitated Diffusion
   **Answer: B**

**9.** Which structure in plant cells is responsible for maintaining turgor pressure?
   C) Chloroplast
   V) Vacuole
   N) Nucleus
   **Answer: C**

**10.** In genetics, what does the term 'phenotype' refer to?
   T) The location of a gene on a chromosome
   **Answer: B**

**11.** Which of the following is a key characteristic of prokaryotic cells?
   P) Presence of a nucleus
   M) Membrane-bound organelles
   C) Complex cytoskeleton
   **Answer: C**

**12.** Which of the following hormones is responsible for the regulation of blood sugar levels?
   A) Adrenaline
   I) Insulin
   T) Thyroxine
   E) Estrogen
   **Answer: B**

**13.** Which term describes the maximum population size that an environment can sustain indefinitely?
   C) Carrying capacity
   B) Biotic potential
   P) Population density
   E) Ecological niche
   **Answer: A**

**14.** In the nitrogen cycle, what process converts nitrogen gas into ammonia?
   N) Nitrogen fixation
   A) Ammonification
   D) Denitrification
   **Answer: B**

**15.** Which of the following cellular processes requires energy to transport molecules against their concentration gradient?
   P) Passive transport
   F) Facilitated diffusion
   A) Active transport
   O) Osmosis
   **Answer: C**
