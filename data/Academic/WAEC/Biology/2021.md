# WAEC Biology Past Questions - 2021

## Questions

**1.** Which of the following processes is primarily responsible for the movement of water and minerals from the roots to the leaves in plants?
   P) Photosynthesis
   T) Translocation
   G) Germination
   **Answer: B**

**2.** In the context of genetics, what is the expected phenotypic ratio in the F2 generation of a monohybrid cross?
   1) 1:2:1
   3) 3:1
   9) 9:3:3:1
   **Answer: B**

**3.** During cellular respiration, which stage produces the most ATP?
   G) Glycolysis
   K) Krebs cycle
   E) Electron transport chain
   L) Lactic acid fermentation
   **Answer: C**

**4.** What is the primary function of the ribosomes in a cell?
   D) DNA replication
   P) Protein synthesis
   L) Lipid synthesis
   E) Energy production
   **Answer: B**

**5.** Which of the following is NOT a characteristic of living organisms?
   G) Growth and development
   A) Ability to evolve
   I) Inability to respond to stimuli
   **Answer: D**

**6.** In the food chain, which level typically has the highest biomass?
   P) Producers
   S) Secondary consumers
   T) Tertiary consumers
   **Answer: C**

**7.** What is the role of enzymes in biological reactions?
   I) Increase activation energy
   D) Decrease activation energy
   C) Chemical equilibrium
   T) Transform substrates to products without specificity
   **Answer: B**

**8.** Which of the following processes is responsible for the variation seen in offspring?
   M) Meiosis
   A) Asexual reproduction
   B) Budding
   **Answer: C**

**9.** What type of immunity is provided by vaccines?
   I) Innate immunity
   P) Passive immunity
   A) Active immunity
   N) Natural immunity
   **Answer: C**

**10.** Which of the following structures is found in prokaryotes but not eukaryotes?
   R) Ribosomes
   P) Plasmids
   M) Mitochondria
   N) Nucleus
   **Answer: B**

**11.** Which of the following elements is a major component of proteins?
   C) Carbon
   H) Hydrogen
   N) Nitrogen
   P) Phosphorus
   **Answer: C**

**12.** In ecological terms, what is a niche?
   T) The genetic variations within a population
   **Answer: B**

**13.** Which anatomical feature is unique to mammals?
   F) Fins
   S) Scales
   M) Mammary glands
   **Answer: C**

**14.** What is the primary function of chloroplasts in plant cells?
   R) Respiration
   P) Photosynthesis
   D) Digestion
   C) Cell division
   **Answer: B**
