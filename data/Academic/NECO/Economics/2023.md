# NECO Economics Past Questions - 2023

## Questions

**1.** The lender of last resort in the banking system is the
   A) industrial bank
   B) mortgage bank
   C) commercial bank
   D) central bank
   **Answer: D**

**2.** In a rapidly growing economy, a change in technology which is labour saving is likely to cause
   A) residual unemployment
   B) structure unemployment
   C) disguised unemployment
   D) frictional unemployment
   **Answer: D**

**3.** Price legislation can be defined as
   A) how price fluctuates in the market
   B) how prices are controlled in the market
   C) how to avoid inflation
   D) how the government fixes the prices of essential commodities.
   **Answer: D**

**4.** When the quantity of a commodity supplied increases and the quantity demanded decreases there will be
   A) a rise in price

   C) price fluctuation
