# JAMB Economics Past Questions - 2020

## Questions

**1.** Which economic model explains the relationship between inflation and unemployment?
   K) Keynesian Model
   P) Phillips Curve
   M) Monetarist Theory
   S) Supply-Side Economics
   **Answer: B**

**2.** What is the primary function of a central bank?
   T) Tax collection
   C) Currency regulation
   P) Public expenditure
   E) Employment creation
   **Answer: B**

**3.** Which of the following is a characteristic of a monopolistic market structure?
   M) Many sellers
   H) Homogeneous products
   P) Perfect information
   **Answer: C**

**4.** What type of goods are characterized by being non-excludable and non-rivalrous?
   P) Public goods
   C) Common resources
   **Answer: B**

**5.** Which of the following best describes opportunity cost?
   T) The total costs of resources
   **Answer: B**

**6.** In which phase of the business cycle is unemployment typically the highest?
   E) Expansion
   P) Peak
   R) Recovery
   **Answer: C**

**7.** Which of the following is an example of a negative externality?
   E) Education
   P) Public parks
   H) Healthcare improvements
   **Answer: B**

**8.** What is fiscal policy primarily concerned with?
   M) Money supply management
   G) Government spending and taxation
   T) Trade balance
   I) Interest rates control
   **Answer: B**

**9.** Which type of inflation results from demand exceeding supply?
   C) Cost-push inflation
   H) Hyperinflation
   D) Demand-pull inflation
   S) Stagflation
   **Answer: C**

**10.** Which of the following economic indicators is considered a leading indicator?
   U) Unemployment rate
   S) Stock market performance
   G) GDP growth
   C) Consumer price index
   **Answer: B**

**11.** The theory of comparative advantage suggests that countries should:
   P) Produce everything they need domestically
   S) Specialize in producing goods they can create most efficiently
   T) Trade only if they are equal in productivity
   A) Avoid trade to support local businesses
   **Answer: B**

**12.** Which of the following is NOT a function of money?
   M) Medium of exchange
   S) Source of employment
   **Answer: D**

**13.** If the demand for a product decreases while supply remains constant, what will happen to equilibrium price?
   I) Increase
   D) Decrease
   R) Remain the same
   B) Become unstable
   **Answer: B**

**14.** What is the main purpose of antitrust laws?
   T) To enforce tariffs
   **Answer: A**
