**1.** If a car runs at a constant speed and takes 4.5 hrs to run a distance of 225 km, what time will it take to run 150 km?
   A)  2 hrs
   B)  4 hrs
   C) 3 hrs
   D) 1 hr
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: Let T be the time taken and D is the distance

For the case given in the problem, the distance covered is directly proportional to the time taken i.e D α T

⇒ D = ST where S is the constant speed

D = 225 km and T = 4.5 hrs

∴ 225 = S x 4.5

S = 225/4.5 = 50 km/hr

When D = 150 km

D = ST

150 = 50 × T

T = 150/50 = 3 hrs

So it will take 3 hrs*
