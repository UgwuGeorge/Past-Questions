**1.** Goodwill can be introduced when

**2.** From the following information: Debtors opening balance is ₦4,000, Debtors closing balance is ₦1,500, Cash received from debtors amounts to ₦8,500, Bad debts written off total ₦350, Discount allowed is ₦500, and Discount received is ₦1,000. What is the amount of sales for the year?
   A) ₦6,850
   B) ₦6,650
   C) ₦3,850
   D) ₦1,550
   **Answer: A**
   *Explanation: Sales = Closing Debtors + Cash Received + Bad Debts Written Off + Discount Allowed - Opening Debtors
Calculation:

Closing Debtors: ₦1,500
Add: Cash received from debtors: ₦8,500
Add: Bad debts written off: ₦350
Add: Discount allowed: ₦500
Less: Opening Debtors: ₦4,000

Sales = ₦1,500 + ₦8,500 + ₦350 + ₦500 - ₦4,000 = ₦6,850*

**21.** The effect of transaction when cash is paid into the bank is?
   A) bank balance decrease and cash balance increase
   B) cash balance decrease and bank balance increase
   C)  bank balance decrease and cash balance decrease

   **Answer: B**
