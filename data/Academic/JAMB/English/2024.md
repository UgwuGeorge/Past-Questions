**1.** Choose the option that best conveys the meaning of the underlined portion in the following sentence;
In the match against the uplanders team, the sub mariners turned out to be the
dark horse

   B) played below their usual form
   C) won unexpectedly
   D) lost as expected
   E) won as expected
   **Answer: C**
