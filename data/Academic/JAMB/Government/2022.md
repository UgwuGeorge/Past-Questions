# JAMB Government Past Questions - 2022

## Questions

**1.** What is the primary purpose of a constitution in a democratic state?
   T) To promote international relations
   **Answer: C**

**2.** Which of the following best describes the concept of 'separation of powers'?
   O) One branch of government controls all others
   D) Different branches have distinct and independent powers
   A) All power is concentrated in the executive branch
   L) Legislative power is shared with the judiciary
   **Answer: B**

**3.** The principle of rule of law implies that:
   T) The law applies only to government officials
   E) Everyone is subject to the law
   L) Laws are arbitrary and can change at will
   O) Only citizens must obey the law
   **Answer: B**

**4.** Which document serves as the supreme law of a country?
   T) The electoral act
   I) International treaties
   **Answer: B**

**5.** What is the significance of political participation?
   I) It reduces the power of civil organizations
   **Answer: B**

**6.** In a capitalist economy, the role of the government is primarily to:
   C) Control all aspects of economic production
   I) Interfere minimally in market operations
   R) Redistribute wealth among citizens
   E) Ensure equal ownership of resources
   **Answer: B**

**7.** Which theory of government suggests that power is derived from the consent of the governed?
   D) Divine Right Theory
   S) Social Contract Theory
   F) Force Theory
   M) Marxist Theory
   **Answer: B**

**8.** What is a key feature of federalism?
   C) Concentration of power in one central government
   D) Distribution of power between national and regional governments
   A) Absence of regional governments
   O) Only one level of government exists
   **Answer: B**

**9.** Which of the following is NOT a function of the political party?
   R) Recruiting candidates for office
   C) Conducting elections
   F) Formulating policies
   E) Establishing monopolies in resource allocation
   **Answer: D**

**10.** The term 'sovereignty' in political science refers to:
   T) The division of power among branches of government
   **Answer: B**

**11.** Which system of government involves a single leader with absolute power?
   D) Democracy
   A) Anarchy
   M) Monarchy
   O) Oligarchy
   **Answer: C**

**12.** What is the primary benefit of a multi-party system?
   I) It minimizes political discourse
   **Answer: B**

**13.** The judiciary is tasked with upholding which of the following principles?
   E) Executive order supremacy
   I) Interpretation and application of law
   L) Legislative authority
   M) Managing public finances
   **Answer: B**

**14.** Which of the following is the highest court in a national judiciary system?
   C) Court of Appeal
   S) Supreme Court
   H) High Court
   M) Magistrate Court
   **Answer: B**
