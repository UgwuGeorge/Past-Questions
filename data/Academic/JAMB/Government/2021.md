# JAMB Government Past Questions - 2021

## Questions

**1.** What is the primary function of the executive branch in a government?
   T) To adjudicate laws
   **Answer: C**

**2.** Which principle is a fundamental aspect of a democratic government?
   D) Disbanding opposition parties
   S) Supremacy of the law
   C) Censorship of the press
   A) Absolute monarchy
   **Answer: B**

**3.** In a federal system of government, power is primarily distributed between?
   T) The president and the cabinet
   C) Central and regional authorities
   J) Judicial and legislative branches
   E) Economic and military sectors
   **Answer: B**

**4.** What document outlines the fundamental principles of a government?
   C) Charter
   B) Bill of Rights
   L) Legislation
   **Answer: A**

**5.** The concept of 'separation of powers' is best defined as?
   T) The division of government responsibilities into distinct branches
   A) Assigning military power to civilian authorities
   C) Centralizing all government functions in one branch
   H) Having only one political party in power
   **Answer: A**

**6.** Which ideology supports the idea of collective ownership of production?
   S) Socialism
   L) Liberalism
   C) Conservatism
   F) Fascism
   **Answer: A**

**7.** What is meant by the term 'judicial review'?
   T) The administration of public trials
   **Answer: C**

**8.** Which of the following is NOT a characteristic of a state?
   P) Population
   S) Sovereignty
   T) Territory
   M) Monopoly on education
   **Answer: D**

**9.** The term 'sovereignty' refers to?
   T) The right of citizens to vote
   E) Equal distribution of power among branches of government
   **Answer: B**

**10.** Which of the following best describes totalitarianism?
   A) A government that values individual freedoms
   **Answer: B**

**11.** What type of government is characterized by rule by the few?
   D) Democracy
   P) Plutocracy
   O) Oligarchy
   M) Monarchy
   **Answer: C**

**12.** The principle of popular sovereignty holds that:
   G) Governments should operate without citizen input
   T) The legitimacy of government comes from the consent of the governed
   E) Elections should be avoided
   L) Leaders can rule by force
   **Answer: B**

**13.** Which document is essential for protecting individual rights in a democracy?
   S) Social contract
   C) Constitutional amendments
   E) Executive orders
   I) International treaties
   **Answer: B**

**14.** Which of the following is a disadvantage of a federal system of government?
   E) Encourages citizen participation
   P) Potential for conflict between national and state governments
   A) Allows for more local governance
   **Answer: B**
