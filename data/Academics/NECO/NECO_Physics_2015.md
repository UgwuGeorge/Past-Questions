**7.** When the downward current flows in a straight vertical conductor, the direction of its magnetic field at a point due north of the wire is?
   A) Upward
   B) North
   C) South
   D) West
   **Answer: D**
   *Explanation: When downward current flows in a vertical conductor, the magnetic field at a point due north of the wire points west (following the right-hand grip rule).*

   A) α- particles and β-rays
   B) α- particles and X-rays
   C) α- particles, β - rays and y-rays

   **Answer: C**
