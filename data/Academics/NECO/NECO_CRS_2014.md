# NECO CRS Past Questions (2014)

## Objectives

**1.** ‘What is this that you have done!”  The people in the ship asked Jonah the question above because
   A) he was fast asleep in the ship
   B) they knew he was fleeing from the LORD
   C) he lied to them about his presence in the ship
   D) they realized that he had made attempts to commit suicide.
   **Answer: B**

**2.** David’s immediate reaction to the news that the hearts of the Israelites have gone after Absalom was to
   A) Weep for Judah
   B) flee to the wilderness
   C) mobilize his army
   D) plead with Absalom
   **Answer: C**

**3.** In Peter’s teaching,” God…………the proud and gives grace to the humble 
   A) receives
   B) relegates
   C) resists
   D) restores
   **Answer: C**

**4.** When Joshua fell upon his face during Israel’s defeat at Ai, God told him to rise and
   A) send more courageous soldiers to Ai
   B) encourage the people
   C) pray fervently against their enemies
   D) sanctify the people
   **Answer: D**

**5.** Let his habitation be made desolate, and let there be no one to live in it’ who is being referred to here?  
   A) King soul
   B) Judas Iscariot
   C) Stephen
   D) Barnabas
   **Answer: B**

**6.** “....... Behold, I will bring evil upon you; I will utterly sweep you away .....” Elijah pronounced destruction on Ahab with the statement above for his sin of
   A) the killing of God’s prophets
   B) worship of other gods
   C) going to war against Ramoth-gilead
   D) greed, covetousness and murder
   **Answer: A**

**7.** “Holy, holy, holy is the Lord of host ....” The statement above was made in reference to the call of
   A) Ezekiel
   B) Jeremiah
   C) Amos
   D) Isaiah
   **Answer: D**

**8.** Solomon showed lack of wisdom when he
   A) put up temples for the gods of his wives
   B) married many wives with children
   C) invited the Queen of Sheba
   D) gave out judgment to two harlots.
   **Answer: A**

**9.** James stated in his epistle that when believers meet various trials, their faith produces
   A) weakness
   B) strength
   C) steadfastness
   D) righteousness
   **Answer: C**

**10.** The incidents that took place following Jesus’ death on the cross did not include the
   A) tearing into two of the temple’s veil.
   B) quaking of the earth that broke the rocks.
   C) the opening of the tombs.
   D) trembling of the chief priest and his conversion.
   **Answer: B**
