**4.** <u>For all he cared: the game was as good as lost</u>
   A) He did not care if the game as lost

   C) He was almost certain that the game would be lost
   D) He was afraid the game would be lost
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: For all I care' is used to say that a person is not worried/ uninterested about what happened to someone or something. 'As good as' indicates a comparison between 'the game' and 'a lost'

Option C is the best answer*
