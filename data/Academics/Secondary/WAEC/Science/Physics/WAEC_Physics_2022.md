**3.** The pinhole camera works on
   A) the principle of the reversibility of light
   B) the principle of the rectilinear propagation of light
   C) Huygen’s principle
   D) the principle of total internal reflection of light
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: A pinhole camera operates on the principle that light travels in straight lines (rectilinear propagation). Light from each point on the object passes through the small hole in a straight line to form an inverted image on the screen opposite the pinhole.*
   *(Note: Closest available year from ALOC database: 2023)*

**4.** The amount of energy required to change a kilogram of ice block into water without a change in temperature is?
   A) specific latent heat of fusion of ice
   B) specific heat capacity of ice
   C) heat capacity of ice
   D) specific heat of vaporization of ice
   **Answer: A**
   *Explanation: Specific latent heat of fusion is the energy required per kilogram to change a substance from solid to liquid at its melting point without temperature change. For ice, this value is approximately 334 kJ/kg or 3.34 × 10⁵ J/kg.*

**5.** A current of 0.5 A flows through a resistor when connected to a 40 V battery. How much energy is dispatched in 2 minutes?
   A) 2400 J
   B) 9600 J
   C) 1500 J
   D) 1200 J
   **Answer: A**
   *Explanation: Given information:

Current, I = 0.5 A
Voltage, V = 40 V
Time, t = 2 minutes = 2 × 60 = 120 s
Energy, E = ?

Solution:
Using the electrical energy formula:
E = VIt
Step 1: Substitute the known values
E = 40 × 0.5 × 120
Step 2: Calculate the energy
E = 40 × 0.5 × 120
E = 20 × 120
E = 2400 J*

**6.** A tuning fork having a frequency of 312 Hz emits a wave which has a wavelength of 1.10 m. Calculate the velocity of sound.
   A) 312.0 ms⁻¹
   B) 110.0 ms⁻¹
   C) 343.2 ms⁻¹
   D) 686.4 ms⁻¹
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: Given information:

Frequency, f = 312 Hz
Wavelength, λ = 1.10 m
Velocity, v = ?

Solution:
Using the wave equation:
v = fλ
Step 1: Substitute the known values
v = 312 × 1.10
Step 2: Calculate the velocity
v = 343.2 m/s*

P = ρgh
Step 1: Substitute the known values
9100 = 2000 × 10 × h
9100 = 20000h
Step 2: Solve for depth
h = 9100/20000
h = 0.455 ...*
