**1.** When the temperature of a liquid increases, its surface tension
   A) decreases
   B) increases
   C) remains constant
   D) increases then decreases
   **Answer: A**
   *Explanation: When the temperature of a liquid increases, its surface tension decreases due to weakening of intermolecular cohesive forces as molecular kinetic energy increases.*

**2.** In voltage measurement, the potentiometer is preferred to voltmeter because it
   A) has a wider range
   B) consumes negligible current
   C) is bulky
   D) responds faster than voltmeter
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: A potentiometer is preferred over a voltmeter for voltage measurements because it does not draw significant current from the circuit being measured.*

**10.** If the rate of evaporation is y, and the rate of condensation is x, then, a vapour is saturated when
   A) x > y
   B) x < y
   C) x = y
   D) x ≤ y
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: A vapour is saturated when the rate of condensation (x) equals the rate of evaporation (y), establishing a dynamic equilibrium where there is no net change in the amount of vapour present.*
