**1.** Which of the following statements about wave motion is correct?
   A) Electromagnetic waves are transverse
   B) Transverse waves do not require a medium for propagation
   C) Longitudinal waves vibrate perpendicular to the direction of travel
   D) All mechanical waves are longitudinal
   **Answer: A**
   *Explanation: Electromagnetic waves are transverse waves with electric and magnetic fields oscillating perpendicular to the direction of propagation.*

**3.** The relative density of a liquid is the ratio of the
   A) Mass of water to the mass of an equal volume of liquid
   B) Upthrust in the liquid to the upthrust in water
   C) Density of water to the density of the liquid
   D) Upthrust in water to the upthrust in the liquid
   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: The relative density equals the ratio of upthrust in the liquid to upthrust in water (which equals the ratio of densities).*
   *(Note: Closest available year from ALOC database: 2016)*

**4.** A ray of light passes through the centre of curvature of a concave mirror and strikes the mirror. At what angle is the ray reflected?
   A) 180°
   B) 90°
   C) 0°
   D) 60°
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: The ray is reflected at 0° because it retraces its path back through the centre of curvature along the same line with no angular deviation.*
   *(Note: Closest available year from ALOC database: 2019)*

**5.** Electrical appliances in homes are normally earthed so that
   A) a person touching the appliances is safe from electric shock
   B) Both the a.c. and d.c. sources can be used
   C) The appliances are maintained at a higher pd than the earth
   D) The appliances are maintained at a lower pd than the earth
   **Answer: A**
   *Explanation: Earthing protects people from electric shock by providing a safe path for fault currents to flow to ground instead of through a person's body.*
   *(Note: Closest available year from ALOC database: 2018)*

**6.** The action of blotting paper on ink is due to
   A) diffusion
   B) osmosis
   C) capillarity
   D) surface tension
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: Capillarity occurs when adhesive forces (between ink and paper fibers) are stronger than cohesive forces (within the ink itself), causing the ink to climb up the tiny spaces in the paper.*
   *(Note: Closest available year from ALOC database: 2017)*

**9.** Which component of fiber-optic connector has a provision of entry for the fibre along with the fixation to connector housing?
   A) coupling device
   B) cable
   C) ferrule
   D) connector housing
   **Answer: C**
   *Explanation: The ferrule is the critical component that holds the optical fiber in place and provides the entry point for it. It fits securely into the connector housing while keeping the fiber aligned for proper light transmission between connected fibers.*
   *(Note: Closest available year from ALOC database: 2022)*

   A) Magnitude and motion
   B) Magnitude and direction
   C) Distance and specified displacement

   **Answer: B**
   *Explanation: A vector quantity has both magnitude and direction*
