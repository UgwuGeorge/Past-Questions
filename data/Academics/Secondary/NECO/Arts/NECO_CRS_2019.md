# NECO CRS Past Questions (2019)

## Objectives

**1.** ‘Now I known that Lord has sent his angel to deliver me ……”who said this? 
   A) Joseph
   B) Moses
   C) Paul
   D) Peter
   **Answer: D**

**2.** The Hebrew midwives who obeyed God rather than Pharaoh were blessed with
   A) favour from all the Israelites
   B) long life in Canaan
   C) resources
   D) families
   **Answer: D**

**3.** The Philippians’ gifts to Paul were for
   A) the growth of the church
   B) his sustenance
   C) distribution to the saints
   D) other Christian prisoners
   **Answer: B**

**4.** God addressed Ezekiel as the “Son of man “. This phrase 
   A) humanity.
   B) character
   C) C.. humility
   D) elegance
   **Answer: A**

**5.** The original name of Shadrach was  
   A) Daniel
   B) Mishael
   C) Hananiah
   D) Azariah
   **Answer: C**

**6.** “I tell you, if these were silent, the very stones would cry out.” Jesus’ statement above was made when
   A) He was rebuked by the Pharisees for eating with sinners
   B) the scribes and the chief priest came to arrest Him
   C) He consoled a woman on His way to the cross
   D) the Pharisees asked Him to rebuke His disciples
   **Answer: D**

**7.** Gehazi became a leper because of his
   A) deceitfulness
   B) greed
   C) wickedness
   D) lies
   **Answer: B**

**8.** ‘Death is swallowed up in victory, “oh death, where is your victory”? This statement is found in the book of
   A) Corinthians.
   B) Romans.
   C) Galatians.
   D) Philemon
   **Answer: A**

**9.** “My son your sins are forgiven”. These were the words of  Jesus to the   
   A) demonia
   B) paralyti
   C) man born blind
   D) centurion’s servant
   **Answer: B**
