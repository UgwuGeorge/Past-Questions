# Bar Finals 2023 - Civil Litigation

## Questions

**1.** Which mode of commencement of action is appropriate where the principal issue is construction of a statute?
   A) Writ of Summons
   B) Originating Summons
   C) Originating Motion
   D) Petition
   **Answer: B**

**2.** A defendant who intends to contest the court's jurisdiction in the Lagos State High Court should file:
   A) A Statement of Defence
   B) A Memorandum of Appearance
   C) A Conditional Appearance under protest
   D) A Motion for adjournment
   **Answer: C**
