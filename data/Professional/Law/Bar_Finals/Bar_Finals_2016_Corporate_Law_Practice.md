# Bar Finals 2016 - Corporate Law Practice

## Questions

**1.** Under CAMA 2020, how many persons are required to form a small company?
   A) At least 2
   B) At least 1
   C) Exactly 7
   D) At least 5
   **Answer: B**

**2.** Which document is required for the registration of a business name in Nigeria?
   A) Memorandum of Association
   B) Articles of Association
   C) Form CAC BN1
   D) Form CAC 1.1
   **Answer: C**
