# MDCN Dental Qualifying Examination May 2022 - Oral Surgery and Pathology

## Questions

**1.** Which appearance on a dental radiograph is characteristic of Ameloblastoma?
   A) Soap-bubble
   B) Cotton-wool
   C) Ground-glass
   D) Sunburst
   **Answer: A**

**2.** Primary local anesthetic for minor oral surgery in controlled hypertension?
   A) Lidocaine 1:100,000 Epi
   B) Mepivacaine plain
   C) Articaine
   D) Prilocaine
   **Answer: A**

**3.** On the Beaufort Scale of wind force, what wind name is given to number 8?
   A) Storm
   B) Breeze
   C) Gale
   D) Hurricane
   **Answer: C**

**4.** Which Landsat Satellite currently has had the longest operational life?
   A) Landsat 6
   B) Landsat 8
   C) Landsat 5
   D) Landsat 7
   **Answer: C**

**5.** Which of the following plastic is commonly used for window frames, gutters and drain pipes?
   A) Polyethylene (PE)
   B) Polyvinylchloride (PVC) 
   C) Polypropylene (PP)
   D) Polystyrene (PS)
   **Answer: B**

**6.** Which scientific unit is named after an Italian nobleman?
   A) Volt
   B) Pascal
   C) Hertz
   D) Ohm
   **Answer: A**

**7.** The word "science" stems from the word "scire" meaning what?
   A) To live
   B) To count
   C) To know
   D) To measure
   **Answer: C**
