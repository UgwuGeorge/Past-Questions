# MDCN Qualifying Examination - Paper III (Pediatrics)

## Questions

**1.** At what age should a child typically be able to sit without support?
   A) 4 months
   B) 6 months
   C) 9 months
   D) 12 months
   **Answer: B**

**2.** Which of the following is the most common cause of neonatal sepsis in Nigeria?
   A) Group B Streptococcus
   B) E. coli
   C) Staphylococcus aureus
   D) Klebsiella species
   **Answer: D**
