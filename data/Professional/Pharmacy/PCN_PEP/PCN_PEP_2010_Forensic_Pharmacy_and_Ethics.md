# PCN PEP 2010 - Forensic Pharmacy and Ethics

## Questions

**1.** According to the PCN Act, the Poison Book must be kept for at least how many years from the last entry?
   A) 1 year
   B) 2 years
   C) 5 years
   D) 10 years
   **Answer: B**

**2.** Which body is responsible for the registration of premises for the sale of medicines in Nigeria?
   A) NAFDAC
   B) PCN
   C) NDLEA
   D) NMA
   **Answer: B**
