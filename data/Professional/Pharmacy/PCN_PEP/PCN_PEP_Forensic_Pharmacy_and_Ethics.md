# PCN PEP - Forensic Pharmacy & Ethics

## Questions

**1.** Which of the following bodies is responsible for the registration of premises for the sale of medicines in Nigeria?
   A) NAFDAC
   B) PCN
   C) NDLEA
   D) NMA
   **Answer: B**

**2.** The Poison and Pharmacy Act (PPA) classifies drugs based on their danger to the public. Which schedule contains Controlled Drugs?
   A) Schedule 1
   B) Schedule 2
   C) Schedule 3
   D) First Schedule
   **Answer: D**
