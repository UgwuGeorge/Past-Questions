# PCN PEP 2006 - Pharmaceutical Care

## Questions

**1.** Pharmaceutical care is best defined as:
   A) Act of dispensing drugs
   B) Patient-centred practice optimising drug therapy outcomes
   C) Manufacturing of drugs
   D) Drug quality control
   **Answer: B**

**2.** The 'Islets of Langerhans' is found in which human organ?
   A) Pancreas
   B) Brain
   C) Kidney
   D) Liver
   **Answer: A**

**3.** Which horizon in a soil profile consists of bedrock?
   A) B
   B) O
   C) D
   D) R
   **Answer: D**

**4.** Which scientific unit is named after an Italian nobleman?
   A) Volt
   B) Ohm
   C) Pascal
   D) Hertz
   **Answer: A**

**5.** What is the molecular formula of Ozone?
   A) O3
   B) N2O
   C) C6H2O6
   D) SO4
   **Answer: A**

**6.** Which of the following is considered classical conditioning?
   A) Schrödinger's cat experiment
   B) Pavlov's dog experiments
   C) Harlow’s monkey experiments
   D) Skinner box experiment
   **Answer: B**
