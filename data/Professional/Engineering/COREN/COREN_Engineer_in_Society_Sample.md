# COREN Professional Interview Questions - Engineer in Society

## Questions

**1.** According to the COREN Act, what is the primary prerequisite for registration as a Professional Engineer in Nigeria?
   A) 5 years of experience
   B) Passing the NSE/COREN professional interview
   C) Having a PhD
   D) Payment of annual dues only
   **Answer: B**

**2.** In project management, 'CPM' stands for:
   A) Cost Project Management
   B) Critical Path Method
   C) Control Process Model
   D) Civil Project Mapping
   **Answer: B**
