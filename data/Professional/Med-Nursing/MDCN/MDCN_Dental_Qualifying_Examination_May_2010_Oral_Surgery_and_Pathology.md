# MDCN Dental Qualifying Examination May 2010 - Oral Surgery and Pathology

## Questions

**1.** Which appearance on a dental radiograph is characteristic of Ameloblastoma?
   A) Soap-bubble
   B) Cotton-wool
   C) Ground-glass
   D) Sunburst
   **Answer: A**

**2.** Primary local anesthetic for minor oral surgery in controlled hypertension?
   A) Lidocaine 1:100,000 Epi
   B) Mepivacaine plain
   C) Articaine
   D) Prilocaine
   **Answer: A**
