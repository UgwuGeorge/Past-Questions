# MDCN Qualifying Examination - Paper II (Obstetrics & Gynecology)

## Questions

**1.** A 28-year-old G1P0 at 34 weeks gestation presents with a blood pressure of 160/110 mmHg and 3+ proteinuria. What is the most likely diagnosis?
   A) Gestational Hypertension
   B) Preeclampsia
   C) Eclampsia
   D) Chronic Hypertension
   **Answer: B**

**2.** Which hormone is primarily responsible for the maintenance of the corpus luteum in early pregnancy?
   A) Estrogen
   B) Progesterone
   C) hCG
   D) LH
   **Answer: C**
