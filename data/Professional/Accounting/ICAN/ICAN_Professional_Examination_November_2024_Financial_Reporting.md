# ICAN Professional Examination November 2024 - Financial Reporting

## Questions

**1.** Which of the following is a fundamental qualitative characteristic of useful financial information per the IFRS Conceptual Framework?
   A) Comparability
   B) Relevance
   C) Timeliness
   D) Understandability
   **Answer: B**

**2.** A company purchased an asset for N100,000 on Jan 1, 2020. Depreciation is 20% reducing balance. What is the NBV at Dec 31, 2021?
   A) N60,000
   B) N80,000
   C) N64,000
   D) N70,000
   **Answer: C**
