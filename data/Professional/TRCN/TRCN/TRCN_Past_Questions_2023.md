# TRCN Professional Qualifying Examination (2023)

## Questions

**1.** Which of these is NOT a professional teaching standard in Nigeria?
   A) Professional Knowledge
   B) Professional Practice
   C) Professional Profit
   D) Professional Values
   **Answer: C**

**2.** The Teachers Registration Council of Nigeria (TRCN) was established by which Act?
   A) Act 31 of 1993
   B) Act 45 of 1990
   C) Act 12 of 2004
   D) Act 5 of 1999
   **Answer: A**
