# TRCN Professional Qualifying Examination (2017)

## Questions

**1.** Who is the primary focus of the teaching-learning process?
   A) Teacher
   B) Learner
   C) Principal
   D) Parent
   **Answer: B**

**2.** The philosopher who believed education should be based on nature was:
   A) Dewey
   B) Rousseau
   C) Plato
   D) Socrates
   **Answer: B**
