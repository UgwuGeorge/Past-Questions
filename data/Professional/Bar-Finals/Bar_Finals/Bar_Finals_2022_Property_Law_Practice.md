# Bar Finals 2022 - Property Law Practice

## Questions

**1.** A deed of assignment of land in Lagos State requires consent of:
   A) The Oba of Lagos
   B) The Local Government Chairman
   C) The Governor of the State
   D) The Attorney General
   **Answer: C**

**2.** The document used to transfer an unexpired term of a lease is known as:
   A) Assent
   B) Deed of Gift
   C) Deed of Assignment
   D) Vesting Order
   **Answer: C**
