# Bar Finals - Civil Litigation

## Questions

**1.** Which of the following modes of commencement of action is appropriate where the principal issue is the construction of a statute?
   A) Writ of Summons
   B) Originating Summons
   C) Originating Motion
   D) Petition
   **Answer: B**

**2.** In the High Court of Lagos State, a defendant who intends to contest the jurisdiction of the court should file:
   A) A Statement of Defence
   B) A Memorandum of Appearance
   C) A Conditional Appearance/Memorandum of Appearance under protest
   D) A Motion for adjournment
   **Answer: C**
